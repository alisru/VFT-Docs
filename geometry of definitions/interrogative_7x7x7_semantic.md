The Foundation: Questoscrapy
----------------------------

The 7x7x7 Interrogative Cube is a **Questoscraper**—a semantic measurement apparatus designed for **Questoscrapy**.

Questoscrapy is the systematic extraction of structured semantic coordinates from unstructured conceptual or volitional content. It extracts the underlying vector structure of an idea by subjecting it to a recursive array of 343 orthogonal interrogative pulses.

### The 7 Planes as Densities of Reality

The whole plane structure originates from **dividing an infinite plane twice**, revealing **7 defined points of intersection**. This geometric division creates the 7 planes and 7 densities of reality.

They function as **relative meters**, measuring the information depth within the universal field. When structured into opposing axis they form the Geometry of Definition in Information Space
