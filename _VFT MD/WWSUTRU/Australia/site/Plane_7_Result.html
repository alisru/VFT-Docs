The Totality of Australian Result

The Totality of Australian Result is the successful creation of a comfortable, fair, and stable society on the edge of an ancient, hostile continent. It produces a Resultant Agent who is quiet, multicultural, coastal, aspirational, and sporting, inheriting the memory of the Digger and completing their identity through citizenship. This agent leaves a Global Footprint of commodities and soft power, dependent on the American alliance while increasingly oriented towards Asia. The Consequence of our definition is a society of home owners and super funds, sustained by Medicare and increasingly powered by the sun, inheriting the biodiversity of Gondwana and enjoying 32 years of unbroken growth. We Evaluate our result through the tension between egalitarianism and aspiration, between extraction and sustainability, between reconciliation and denial. We Judge through the vote, the royal commission, the court, the media, the stats, the pub test, and the budget, using every method available to verify our claims. The Future is uncertain—the energy transition, the republic, the treaty, the Asian century—but the goal is clear: preserve the Good Life for those who come after us. Ultimately, the Final Word is Australia itself: a fair go, a good lifestyle, an island girt by sea, offering a second chance, murmuring "she'll be right," grateful for the land, and hoping to remain one and free.
