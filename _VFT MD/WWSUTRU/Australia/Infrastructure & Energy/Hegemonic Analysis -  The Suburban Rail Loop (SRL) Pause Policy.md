### The Mathematical Proof of the Lie ("Back of Napkin" Math)

To demonstrate the project's insolvency, we apply basic arithmetic to the PBO's own figures.

- **Total Cost (Construction + Ops):** \~\$216 Billion (East & North only).

- **Lifecycle:** 50 Years.

- **Daily Cost to Break Even:** \$216,000,000,000 / 50 years / 365 days = **\$11.8 million per day**.

- **Revenue Reality:** Even assuming a generous 400,000 daily trips (highly optimistic for an orbital line), the cost per trip is \~\$29.50.

- **Ticket Cap:** Public transport fares are capped (currently \~\$10.60 daily).

- **The Deficit:** Every single passenger trip represents a direct taxpayer subsidy of \~\$19.00. This is not "public service"; it is **financial hemorrhage**. The project is a permanent liability engine designed to transfer \$11.8 million *per day* from the public purse to the debt holders and operators.
