> **Plane 4 Totality Judgment:**
> Australian Drive produces a **strongly positive** moral vector in its core expressions. The plane is defined by the **Volunteer** archetype and the pursuit of the **Fair Go**—both peak expressions of proactive universal benefit. First Nations perspectives (**Country**, **Resistance**, **Abundance**, **Guilt**) consistently provide moral correction to settler narratives. The plane is haunted by the **Bludger** (leech), **Cultural Cringe** (self-doubt), and the **Knocker** (leveler). The "Greatest Lies" cluster around **Tall Poppy Syndrome** (punishing excellence), **Sledging** (psychological dominance), and **Gambling** (speculative exploitation). The Result section shows a stable but **passive** outcome—**Middle Class** conformity, **Suburban** retreat, and risk-averse **Stability**.
