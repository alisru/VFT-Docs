> **Plane 6 Totality Judgment:**
> Australian Foundation is the plane of **maximum moral tension**. It contains both the highest positive vectors (**Mabo**, **Living Wage**, **Secret Ballot**, **The Dreaming**) and the most extreme negative vectors (**Terra Nullius**, **White Australia**, **The Colonizer**, **Norfolk Island**). The plane is defined by the struggle between the **Original Sin** (dispossession, erasure, exclusion) and the **Capacity for Correction** (Mabo, Uluru return, Unfinished Business). First Nations perspectives (**Indigenous Elder**, **Warrior**, **Fire-Stick Farming**, **The Dreaming**) consistently provide the moral anchor and correction to settler narratives. The plane demonstrates that Australia was built on a lie but possesses the legal and moral capacity to acknowledge and repair that lie. The **Unfinished Business** (Uluru Statement) represents the active moral debt that demands ongoing response.
