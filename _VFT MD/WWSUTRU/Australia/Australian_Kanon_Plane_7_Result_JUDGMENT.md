> **Totality Judgment:**
> Australian Result produces a **net positive** moral vector, pulled toward Greater Good by institutions (Medicare, the Vote, Royal Commissions) and aspirations (Energy Transition, Treaty, Fair Go). However, it carries irreducible shadows: the **Dispossession** of First Nations, the **extractive economy**, the **strategic dependence** on America, and the **Aspirational** selfishness that erodes solidarity. The "Lucky Country" critique remains valid: too much passive inheritance, not enough active creation. The future judgment depends on whether Australia moves toward Treaty, Transition, and Republic, or regresses into Denial, Extraction, and Dependence.
