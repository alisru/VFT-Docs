> **Plane 3 Totality Judgment:**
> Australian Land produces a **mixed** moral vector. Its highest expressions are the **First Nations perspectives** (Custodianship, Songlines) and **heroic infrastructure** (Flying Doctor, Pipeline, Dam, Telegraph). The plane is deeply haunted by **Terra Nullius** (the greatest lie), the **Squatter** seizure of land, and modern **extraction** methods (Opencut, Uranium) that violate ancient taboos. The climate and ecology are largely neutral forces requiring adaptation. The Result section shows a civilization that has **retreated** to the coastal rim, pursuing **hedonistic comfort** (Lifestyle, BBQ) while maintaining **exclusionary** borders (Quarantine)—yet facing an inevitable **Future** of Asian integration.
