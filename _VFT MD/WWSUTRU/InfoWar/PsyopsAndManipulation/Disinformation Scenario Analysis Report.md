## Section 1: The Geopolitical Substrate – The Minimisation Plan and Rhizomatic War

To understand the specific mechanics of the 'Pedo Jesus' and 'Inverse Coup' narratives, one must first understand the strategic theatre in which they operate. The conflict is defined by the doctrine of "Rhizomatic War," a concept adapted from post-structuralist philosophy to military strategy.^4^ Unlike hierarchical, state-on-state warfare, rhizomatic conflict is non-linear, decentralized, and subterranean. It spreads horizontally through networks of influence, exploiting existing societal fissures—economic inequality, racial tension, and the erosion of trust in elites—much like a root system permeates soil.^1^

### 1.1 The "Minimisation Plan" and the Sino-Russian Axis

The overarching grand strategy driving these phenomena is the "Minimisation Plan." This multi-domain approach is attributed to a strategic convergence between Moscow and Beijing, united by a shared objective: to render the Western democratic model "chaotic, corrupt, and unworkable in the public imagination".^1^ The goal is not necessarily to prove the superiority of authoritarianism, but to destroy the viability of its alternative.

This strategy inverts the Gramscian concept of hegemony. Where Gramsci described how a ruling class maintains power by manufacturing consent, the Minimisation Plan seeks to establish "anti-hegemony" by manufacturing dissent and poisoning the epistemological foundations of society.^1^ The primary weapon is the "Reputation Flip," a campaign of ideological warfare designed to transform the United States from the "leader of the free world" into the "objective evil" in the eyes of its own citizens and the global community.^1^

### 1.2 Delusionism: The Philosophical Engine of Chaos

The operational core of this strategy is "Delusionism." Traditional propaganda seeks to replace one truth with another. Delusionism, however, seeks to annihilate the concept of truth itself. It actively rejects the existence of a single, verifiable objective reality in favor of multiple, competing, and malleable narratives.^1^

The desired end-state is "Epistemic Nihilism".^5^ In this cognitive condition, the target populace becomes so overwhelmed by contradictory data, sensationalist claims, and the blurring of fact and fiction that they abandon the search for objective truth entirely. They retreat into tribalism, accepting narratives based on emotional resonance rather than empirical evidence. This state of epistemic exhaustion is the prerequisite for the 'Inverse Citizens Coup,' as a citizenry that cannot agree on the basic facts of reality cannot coordinate to defend its institutions.^6^ The strategy weaponizes "narrative gravity," pulling disparate and often contradictory conspiracy theories into a dense, self-sustaining "memeplex" that becomes impervious to external fact-checking.^7^
