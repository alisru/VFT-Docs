# Attraction Tensor Questions: The Epstein Files

**Purpose:** To gauge public sentiment, identify ideological capture, and measure the \"Hum\" of cognitive dissonance surrounding the potential release of the Epstein lists. These questions are designed to test faith in justice, susceptibility to partisan framing, and the readiness to accept \"manufactured decay.\"

### 1. The \"How\" Vector (Process & Integrity)

**Focus:** Testing faith in the mechanism of evidence.

- **Question:** \"Why does everyone trust that Epstein hasn\'t just written whoever into his logs to incriminate innocents?\"

  - **Maximiser Response:** \"We don\'t trust logs blindly. We trust *corroboration*. Flight logs must match pilot records, victim testimonies, and financial trails. Incrimination requires cross-referenced proof, not just a name in a book.\"

  - **Minimiser/Cynic Response:** \"Exactly. It\'s all leverage. He probably wrote down names of people he *wanted* to blackmail just to muddy the waters. The whole list is poisoned fruit; you can\'t trust any of it.\" (Preparation for dismissal of evidence).

- **Question:** \"How do we distinguish between a \'flight\' and a \'crime\'?\"

  - **Test:** Does the subject conflate presence with guilt, or do they demand context?

### 2. The \"What\" Vector (Impact & Reaction)

**Focus:** Testing the expectation of social stability vs. chaos.

- **Question:** \"Why does everyone trust that MAGA won\'t freak out when innocents are framed in the files?\"

  - **Maximiser Response:** \"If the evidence is weak, the legal system should exonerate them. Reactions should be based on the *quality* of proof, not the tribal affiliation of the accused.\"

  - **Minimiser/Cynic Response:** \"Of course they will freak out. It doesn\'t matter if they are innocent or guilty; if a name is on there, it\'s war. The truth doesn\'t matter, only the headline.\" (Acceptance of post-truth tribalism).

- **Question:** \"What happens if the list contains equal numbers of Democrats and Republicans?\"

  - **Test:** Does the subject expect a \"balanced\" purge, or do they believe one side will be protected while the other is persecuted?

### 3. The \"Who\" Vector (The Agents)

**Focus:** Testing trust in the custodians of the information.

- **Question:** \"Who benefits most if the files are released in a redacted or \'drip-feed\' manner?\"

  - **Maximiser Response:** \"No one benefits from drip-feeding except those trying to hide. The public benefits from full, immediate transparency to rip the band-aid off.\"

  - **Minimiser/Cynic Response:** \" The media and the Deep State. They will drip it out to control the news cycle and distract us from \[Current Event X\].\" (Conspiratorial alignment).

- **Question:** \"Why do we assume the \'Real\' clients are even on the list?\"

  - **Test:** Does the subject believe the *true* power brokers are above documentation? (Deep State cynicism).

### 4. The \"Why\" Vector (Motive & Leverage)

**Focus:** Testing the understanding of the system\'s purpose.

- **Question:** \"Why was such a massive leverage operation allowed to run for so long?\"
