## Summary: Plane 1 Moral Vectors

### Highest Positive Vectors (Greater Good):
1. **Self-Evidence** (+1.0, +0.9) — The pre-state sovereign axiom.
2. **Merit** (+1.0, +0.8) — True equality of character evaluation.
3. **Recognition** (+1.0, +0.8) — Total expansion of the franchise.
4. **Justice** (+1.0, +0.7) — Core systemic re-alignment.

### Most Negative/Subdued Vectors (Stasis / Constraint):
*(Note: Unlike the Australian Kanon, the official American Identity Plane 1 contains no explicitly mapped "Greatest Lies" or deep negative moral valences in its core positive articulation, representing a highly idealized architectural blueprint. Stasis vectors represent required structural friction.)*
1. **Civilian Control** (+1.0, -0.9) — High moral value achieved through near-total suppression of military will.
2. **Humility** (+0.8, -0.8) — Submission of certainty to societal function.
3. **The Great Soul** (+1.0, -0.6) — Massive moral weight achieved through the stillness of the dead.

### High Will / Ambition Vectors (The Engine):
1. **The Pursuit of Happiness** (+0.7, +1.0) — The infinite forward vector.
2. **Independence** (+0.8, +1.0) — Absolute systemic severance.

---

> **Plane 1 Totality Judgment:**
> American Identity produces an overwhelmingly **net positive and highly proactive** moral vector. Its architecture is characterized by extremely high Will (+ψ) expressing itself as constant motion, ingenuity, and self-reliance, anchored by absolute moral axioms (Self-Evidence, Merit, Justice). Where it dips into negative Will (-ψ), it does so deliberately to create structural safety (Civilian Control, Checks and Balances) rather than cultural complacency. It is an Identity engineered for maximum velocity, governed only by the deliberate friction of its own design.
