## Summary: Plane 6 Moral Vectors

### Highest Positive Vectors (Greater Good):
1. **Natural Rights / The Constitution / Separation of Powers / Amendment** (+1.0, -0.9 to +0.8) — The supreme structural brilliance of the Founding Concepts, combining absolute individual moral sovereignty with extremely rigid legal constraints on the government.
2. **The Revolutionary / Self-Determination / Liberty** (+0.9 to +1.0, +0.9 to +1.0) — The high-velocity rejection of tyranny and the total assertion of the right to rule the self.
3. **The Immigrant** (+1.0, +0.9) — The perpetual renewal engine of the Founding Idea.
4. **Hope** (+1.0, +0.9) — The messianic optimism of the American experiment serving as the pilot program for humanity.

### Highest Tension & Negative Vectors (Greater Evils / Greatest Lies):
1. **The Slave / The Indigenous** (-0.9, -0.8 / -0.7, -0.5) — *Greater Evils.* The profound moral hypocrisy embedded in the very foundation of the Republic—building an empire of Liberty precisely on top of stolen bodies and stolen land.
2. **War** (-0.2, +1.0) — *Greatest Lie.* The horrific kinetic reality required to forcibly execute high ideals; the required baptism of blood to birth peace.

### Structurally Suppressive Vectors (Lesser Goods):
The Plane of Foundation is dominated by structurally dampening constraints designed to prevent tyranny and idolization of the present:
*   **Federalism / Separation of Powers** (+0.9, -0.6 / +1.0, -0.9)
*   **The Precedent / Originalism / Civil Religion** (+0.5 to +0.7, -0.6 to -0.7)

---

> **Plane 6 Totality Judgment:**
> Plane 6 is the **Armor of the Idea**. It exhibits extremely high **Morality (+υ)** but relies heavily on suppressive and static **Will (-ψ)** to safeguard the system. The Founding generation (The Framers) deliberately engineered a machine that runs slowly, separating powers, demanding compromises, and forcing interpretation of precedent precisely to stop the hot, tyrannical engine of the State from catching fire. Its brilliant, transcendent ideas—**Liberty, Reason, Natural Rights**—are inextricably bonded to the catastrophic **Greater Evils** of slavery and indigenous displacement. America is an idea that conquered reality, but reality charged a terrible moral price for the land. Its legacy is a heavy burden of Continuity: a civil religion where we worship imperfect architects who built a perfect machine.
