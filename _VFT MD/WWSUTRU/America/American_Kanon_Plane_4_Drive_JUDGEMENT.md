## Summary: Plane 4 Moral Vectors

### Highest Positive Vectors (Greater Good):
1. **Life / Liberty / Duty / Truth / Love** (+1.0, +0.6 to +0.9) — The core philosophical values operate entirely in the Greater Good, providing the moral foundation for the nation's energy.
2. **The Immigrant** (+0.9, +1.0) — Maximum volition replacing passive birthright.
3. **Freedom** (+1.0, +0.9) — The mechanism for unstoppable human ascent.
4. **Happiness** (+0.9, +1.0) — Unyielding pursuit of the self-defined Good.

### Highest Tension & Negative Vectors (Greatest Lies):
1. **Greed** (-0.6, +1.0) — The dark, consumptive inversion of ambition.
2. **Stress / Burnout** (-0.5 / -0.4, +0.8 / +0.9) — The psychological collapse inherent in an engine lacking an "off" switch.
3. **The Winner** (-0.3, +1.0) — Total moral compromise justified by the final outcome.
4. **Marketing** (-0.3, +0.9) — Psychological manipulation to engineer false wants.

---

> **Plane 4 Totality Judgment:**
> Plane 4 represents the raw **Engine** of the American system. It records the highest average **Will (+ψ)** of any plane thus far. Driven by fear (Scarcity/Guilt) and pulled by promise (Abundance/Freedom), the American Agent is an unstoppable vector of momentum. The Morality (+υ) frequently slips from transcendent Greater Good (Love, Truth) down into the purely instrumental (Self-Interest, Competition) and routinely crosses into the **Greatest Lie** when high velocity overrides human limits (Burnout, Stress, Greed, Winner-Takes-All). It is a machine running incredibly hot, achieving global dominance at the cost of profound internal neurosis.
