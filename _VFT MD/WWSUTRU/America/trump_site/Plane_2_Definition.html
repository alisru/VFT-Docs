On the Plane of Definition, Donald Trump continues the pattern established in Identity: he maximizes the vectors of Individualism, Unbound Will, and Aggressive Capitalism (The Individual, The Consumer, The Celebrity, The Internet, Pursuit of Happiness, Grievance, Litigation). He is the ultimate "Free Man" and "Consumer" operating within the American system.

However, Plane 2 is defined heavily by structural constraints and procedural fairness designed to protect the Republic from tyranny. Here, Trump's scores plummet. He is systematically hostile to Procedural Restraint, Institutional Truth, and Shared Power (The Constitution, Democracy, Republic, Law, Justice, Due Process, The Press, Debate). The American Definition demands that the raw energy of the individual submit to the impersonal architecture of the Law and the Constitution; Trump's core political project is precisely the refusal of that submission.
