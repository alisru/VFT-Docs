Plane Totality Analysis

Donald Trump vs. American Method

Plane 5 Score: -2 / 49
Plane 5 Percentage: 48.0% Alignment (Based on 14/49 possible +1s, adjusting for neutral 0s)

Analysis:
On the Plane of Method, Trump's scores drop sharply compared to Plane 3 and 4. The American 'How' is intensely grounded in Empiricism, Engineering, Optimization, Data, and Truth (The Laboratory, The Algorithm, Efficiency, Empiricism, Proof). Trump violently rejects these core epistemological vectors. He operates on instinct, chaos, and narrative, making him fundamentally anti-empirical. He is the ultimate enemy of The Professional and The Technocrat.

However, he scores highly wherever the Method relies on Raw Capitalism, Disruption, Marketing, and Scale (The Entrepreneur, Wall Street, Profit, Scale, Capitalism).

Most revealingly, Trump scores a near-perfect +1 sweep on Plane 5.7 (The Effect of the Method). He is the ultimate avatar of American Materialism, The Consumer, Affluence, and Pollution. The Kanon identifies these as the tragic, hollowing (negative) byproducts of the American Method. Trump does not just navigate these negative exhaust fumes; he embodies them and sells them back to the populace as the definition of success.
