Donald Trump vs. American Land

Plane 3 Score: +15 / 49
Plane 3 Percentage: 65.3% Alignment (Based on 21/49 possible +1s, adjusting for neutral 0s)

Analysis:
Plane 3 (Land) is where Donald Trump finds his strongest alignment with the American Kanon thus far. As a real estate developer, his core operating system aligns perfectly with the Mechanized, Extractive, and Commodified vectors of the American Map. He scores exceptionally high on The City, The Resources, Property, Manifest Destiny, Sovereignty, and The Market. He perfectly channeled the grievance of The Ruin (Rust Belt) and weaponized Nostalgia.

His failures on this plane are entirely related to Stewardship, Nature, and Shared Space (The Native, The Wilderness, Conservation, Sanctuary, Climate Change). He views the Land exclusively as a battery to be drained, a brand to be labeled, or a fortress to be walled. He embodies the high-Will (+ψ) conquest of the geography, but completely rejects the moral (+υ) constraints of ecological reality or international agreements (The Treaty).
