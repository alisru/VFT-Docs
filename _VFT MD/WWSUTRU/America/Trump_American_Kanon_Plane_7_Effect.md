**Analysis:**
Plane 7 represents the Final Judgments and the ultimate outputs of the American Kanon. Here, Donald Trump scores positively only on vectors related to **Material Apex, Communication, and Raw Will** (The Internet, The Moon Landing, The Pursuit, Renewal, Wealth Inequality, Hubris). He successfully harnesses the chaotic energy of the system's exhaust (The Consumer, The Cynic, Rock and Roll rebellion).

However, he vehemently opposes the **Moral, Structural, and Unifying Results** of the American experiment. He attacks the foundational outputs that keep the Republic stable: **The Vote, The Court, The Media, E Pluribus Unum, The City on a Hill**. Furthermore, he actively accelerates the "Greatest Lies" of the system (Climate Crisis, Hubris, Meaning vs Materialism), pouring gas on the fires that threaten the host. 

Ultimately, Trump rejects the final American equation (**The United States, E Pluribus Unum**). He does not wish to integrate the Many into the One; he wishes to dominate the Many so that only He remains.
