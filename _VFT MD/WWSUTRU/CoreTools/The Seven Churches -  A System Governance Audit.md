### **SECTION III — THE OTHER STATES**

#### **1. PERGAMOS (The Seat of Power)**

- **System State:** **The Intelligence State.**

- **Analysis:** It dwells "where Satan's throne is." This represents the **Center of Control**. In a geopolitical context, this is the nation or city where the "Minimisation Plan" is headquartered. The error of Pergamos is **Compromise**—trying to coexist with the Beast system (eating things sacrificed to idols) rather than purging it.

#### **2. LAODICEA (The End State)**

- **System State:** **Late-Stage Capitalism / The Compliant.**

- **Analysis:** "I am rich, have become wealthy, and have need of nothing." This is the **Delusion of Materialism**. The system believes wealth = health.

- **The Diagnosis:** "You are wretched, miserable, poor, blind, and naked." The system has **Zero Moral Assets**. It is "Blind" (cannot see the Minimisation Plan) and "Naked" (has no Robe/Identity of its own).

- **The Threat:** "I will vomit you out of My mouth." Total System Rejection. The Universe cannot tolerate a vacuum of Will. Apathy is deleted.

#### **3. PHILADELPHIA (The Target State)**

- **System State:** **The Maximiser Society.**

- **Analysis:** It has "Little Strength" (It does not rely on authoritarian force/military might). Yet, it has "Kept My Word" (Maintained the Logic/Logos).

- **The Reward:** "I have set before you an open door, and no one can shut it." This is **Unrestricted Possibility (**\$\\Pi\$**).** Because the system is trusted with Truth, it is granted full access to the Future.

- **The Vindication:** The "Synagogue of Satan" (The Liars) are forced to come and worship at their feet. The Minimisers are forced to acknowledge that the Maximiser system is the only valid one.
