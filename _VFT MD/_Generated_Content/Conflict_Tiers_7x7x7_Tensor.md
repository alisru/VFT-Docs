# Conflict Tiers 7×7×7 Tensor Mapping

## I. The Canonical planes (The Grammar of Reality)

As per the official 7×7×7 meaning rules, every address in this tensor is constructed from the following 7 planes:

1.  **Q1: WHO** — Metaphysical (Will & Direction)
2.  **Q2: WHAT** — Possible (Faith & Probability)
3.  **Q3: WHERE** — Physical (Matter & Distance)
4.  **Q4: WHY** — Lyrical (Meaning & Resonance)
5.  **Q5: HOW** — Logical (Count & Consistency)
6.  **Q6: CAUSE** — Historical (Sequence & Causality)
7.  **Q7: EFFECT** — Emotive (Passion & Consequence)

## II. Primary Address (Q): The Tiers of Conflict

The Q-layer defines the **Scale and Domain** of the conflict.

-   **Q1 — Tier 1: Personal Synthesis (The Gun)**
    *   *Interrogative*: Who is the conflict? (The individual actor).
-   **Q2 — Tier 2: Crew-Scale Synthesis (Artillery)**
    *   *Interrogative*: What can the conflict do? (Positional potentiality/Crew).
-   **Q3 — Tier 3: Impersonal Synthesis (The Bomb)**
    *   *Interrogative*: Where does the conflict land? (Physical mass impact/Area denial).
-   **Q4 — Tier 4: Automated Synthesis (The Missile)**
    *   *Interrogative*: Why does the conflict hit? (Lyrical precision/Automated meaning).
-   **Q5 — Tier 5: Existential Synthesis (The Nuke)**
    *   *Interrogative*: How does the conflict end? (Logical finality/Existential paradox).
-   **Q6 — Tier 6: Abstract Synthesis (Economic & Ideological Warfare)**
    *   *Interrogative*: What caused the conflict? (Historical obfuscation/Siege).
-   **Q7 — Tier 7: Transcendent Synthesis (Truth Warfare)**
    *   *Interrogative*: What is the effect of the conflict? (Emotive Aletheia/Unconcealment).
