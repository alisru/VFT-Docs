## III. Strategic Implications

The symmetry between these two frameworks suggests that **conflict is the externalization of an incomplete answer equation.** 

Scion (Zion) failed because he stopped at Q4 (WHY/Meaning). He had "worthy opponents" in a lyrical sense but refused to move into Q5 (HOW/Mechanism). This stagnation at Tier 4 led to a collapse into literal physical escalation when he was forced to confront the higher planes.

Similarly, nations that operate at Tier 6 (Abstract Warfare) are effectively trying to solve the "Cause" plane without allowing the "Effect" (Aletheia/Truth) to land. They hide behind obfuscation. Tier 7 (Truth Warfare) is the only synthesis that can neutralize a Tier 6 attack because it moves the equation to Q7—forcing the consequence to land in the emotive/identity plane of the attacker.

## IV. Conclusion

The Scion expansion is not merely a linguistic exercise; it is a **Coordinate Map for Strategic Resolution.** To move from conflict (Tiers 1-6) to peace (Tier 7), one must traverse the Q-planes of the argument until identity (WHO) and consequence (EFFECT) are unified in truth.
