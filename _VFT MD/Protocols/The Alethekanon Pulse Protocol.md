# The Alethekanon Pulse Protocol: A Manual for Truth Detection

**Version:** 2.2 (The Fractal Plane Integration)

**Formula:** $A_0 \rightarrow Q \rightarrow A_1 \rightarrow q \rightarrow A_2 \rightarrow c \rightarrow A_{final}$

## 0. The Definitions (The Variables)

To navigate reality, we must define our terms. We use two specific lists
to map the territory.

### Variable A: The State (The 7 Frames)

When we say **A** (Assumption, Answer, Verdict), we are locating the
idea in one of these 7 specific Reality Frames.

1.  **Natural State (0):** Raw, neutral data. (Zero Point).

2.  **Good Truth (1):** Reality + Benefit.

3.  **Bad Truth (2):** Reality + Suffering.

4.  **Good Lie (3):** Illusion + Benefit. (The Trap).

5.  **Bad Lie (4):** Illusion + Suffering. (The Weapon).

6.  **Good Preference (5):** Subjective Like.

7.  **Bad Preference (6):** Subjective Dislike.

### Variables Q, q, c: The Geometry (The 7 Planes)

When we Ask (**Q**), Sense (**q**), or Act (**c**), we must target the
specific Plane of Reality defined by the Fractal Ratio Protocol.

1.  **Who** (The Filter Vector)
    $\rightarrow$ **The Meta-Physical Plane**

    - *Target:* Identity, Bias, Intent.

    - *Sensor:* Willpower (Directional Time).

2.  **Where** (The Locus Vector)
    $\rightarrow$ **The Physical Plane**

    - *Target:* Context, Coordinates, Territory.

    - *Sensor:* Locality (Distance).

3.  **What** (The Definition Vector)
    $\rightarrow$ **The Possible Plane**

    - *Target:* Probability, Risk, Substance.

    - *Sensor:* Verification (Faith/Risk).

4.  **Why** (The Drive Vector)
    $\rightarrow$ **The Lyrical Plane**

    - *Target:* Resonance, Meaning, Emotional Drive.

    - *Sensor:* Resonance (Non-Euclidean Time).

5.  **How** (The Method Vector)
    $\rightarrow$ **The Logical Plane**

    - *Target:* Validity, Consistency, Process.

    - *Sensor:* Computation (Process Time).

6.  **Cause** (The Origin Vector)
    $\rightarrow$ **The Historical Plane**

    - *Target:* Lineage, Memory, Causal Chain.

    - *Sensor:* Sequence (Linear Time).

7.  **Effect** (The Impact Vector)
    $\rightarrow$ **The Emotive Plane**

    - *Target:* Strain, Harmony, Global Trust.

    - *Sensor:* Strain (Energetic Time).

## Step 1: $A_0$ - The Gut Check (The Prior State)

### Plain English: \"Where am I starting?\"

You never start at zero. You start with a bias.

- **The Rule:** Identify your starting **A-State**.

- **Action:** \"I am starting in **Bad Preference** (I hate this guy)\"
  or \"I am starting in **Good Lie** (I hope this scam is real).\"

### Technical: The Prior Probability ($P(H)$)

This establishes the initial coordinate on the 7-State Map. If you do
not admit you are in a \"Preference\" state, you will mistake it for a
\"Truth\" state later.

## Step 2: $Q$ - The Grid (The Inquiry Vectors)

### Plain English: \"Build the Trap.\"

We build a cage using the 7 Vectors to ensure the truth cannot escape.

- **Action:** Run the **Q-Vectors** on their Planes:

  - **Q-Who (Meta-Physical):** \"Who is the actor and what is their
    Intent?\"

  - **Q-Why (Lyrical):** \"Does the story resonate or feel forced?\"

  - **Q-How (Logical):** \"Is the method valid and reproducible?\"

  - *(Repeat for all 7)*.

### Technical: Vector Space Definition

This is the **Dimensionality Setup**. You are defining the coordinate
system ($x, y, z, t, \psi, \upsilon$) for the inquiry.

## Step 3: $A_1$ - The Logic Check (Alignment)

### Plain English: \"Is my trap rigged?\"

Stop. Look at your **Q-Vectors**.

- **The Test:** Did you ask the questions in a way that forces the
  answer to match your
  $A_0$?

  - *Rigged:* asking \"Why is he stupid?\" (Presumes
    $A_0$).

  - *Clean:* asking \"What is his IQ?\" (Seeks Natural State).

### Technical: The Framing Ratio ($A/Q$)

Tests for Circular Reasoning. The Inquiry
($Q$) must be independent of the Assumption
($A$).

## Step 4: $q$ - The Senses (The Data Fill)

### Plain English: \"Fill the Trap.\"

Now we pour raw data into the 7 Vectors we built in Step 2.

- **Action:** Fill the **q-Vectors**:

  - **q-Who:** The specific names/IDs.

  - **q-Where:** The GPS coordinates/URLs.

  - **q-Why:** The stated vs. actual motives.

  - *(Repeat for all 7)*.

### Technical: The Natural State Fill

This is the **Observational Layer**. We populate the vector slots with
raw values.

## Step 5: $A_2$ - The Reality Check (The Delta)

### Plain English: \"The Shock.\"

Compare $A_0$ (Your Starting State) with
$q$ (The Filled Vectors).

- **The Test:** Does the **q-Data** support your
  $A_0$?

  - *Scenario:* You started in **Good Truth**
    ($A_0$). The **q-Vectors** show deception
    and harm.

  - *The Shock:* You are actually in **Good Lie**.

- **Action:** Calculate the gap. Move your position on the 7-State Map.

### Technical: The Delta Calculation ($\Delta = |A_0 - q|$)

If $\Delta > 0$, your internal map
($A$) does not match the external territory
($q$). You must move.

## Step 6: $c$ - The Utility (The Action Vectors)

### Plain English: \"What do I do with this?\"

Now that you have the Truth
($A_2$), you must act. We use the same 7 Vectors
to design the action.

- **Action:** Define the **c-Vectors**:

  - **c-Who:** \"Who needs to do it?\" (Me? Us?)

  - **c-Where:** \"Where do I apply force?\"

  - **c-How:** \"What method do I use?\"

### Technical: The Utility Function ($U(x)$)

This determines the **Vector of Application**. It converts Knowledge
($A_2$) into Kinetic Energy
($c$).

## Step 7: $A_{final}$ - The Verdict (The Synthesis)

### Plain English: \"The New State.\"

This is your final resting place on the map.

- **The Output:** \"I started in **Good Lie**
  ($A_0$). I checked the **Vectors**
  ($Q, q$). I found the **Natural State**. I
  applied **Action**
  ($c$). I have arrived at **Good Truth**
  ($A_{final}$).\"

### Technical: The Posterior Probability ($P(H|E)$)

The iteration is complete. The $A_{final}$ of this loop becomes the $A_0$ (Prior) of the next loop.

## Summary of the Flow

1.  $A_0$**:** \"I like this. It feels good.\" (State: **Good Preference**)
2.  $Q$**:** \"Who (Meta-Physical) / How (Logical)?\" (The 7 Vectors)
3.  $A_1$**:** \"Am I asking fair questions?\" (Logic Check)
4.  $q$**:** \"The data shows it harms others.\" (The 7 Vectors Filled)
5.  $A_2$**:** \"Oh. It feels good to me, but hurts them.\" (Shift to State: **Greatest Lie**)
6.  $c$**:** \"I must stop supporting it.\" (Action Vectors)
7.  $A_{final}$**:** \"I see it for what it is.\" (State: **Bad Truth**)
