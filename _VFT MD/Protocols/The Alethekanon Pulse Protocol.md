## 0. The Definitions (The Variables)

To navigate reality, we must define our terms. We use two specific lists to map the territory.

### Variable A: The State (The 7 Frames)

When we say **A** (Assumption, Answer, Verdict), we are locating the idea in one of these 7 specific Reality Frames.

1.  **Natural State (0):** Raw, neutral data. (Zero Point).

2.  **Good Truth (1):** Reality + Benefit.

3.  **Bad Truth (2):** Reality + Suffering.

4.  **Good Lie (3):** Illusion + Benefit. (The Trap).

5.  **Bad Lie (4):** Illusion + Suffering. (The Weapon).

6.  **Good Preference (5):** Subjective Like.

7.  **Bad Preference (6):** Subjective Dislike.

### Variables Q, q, c: The Geometry (The 7 Planes)

When we Ask (**Q**), Sense (**q**), or Act (**c**), we must target the specific Plane of Reality defined by the Fractal Ratio Protocol.

1.  **Who** (The Filter Vector) ![](media/image11.png){width="0.20182305336832895in" height="0.24431211723534557in"} **The Meta-Physical Plane**

    - *Target:* Identity, Bias, Intent.

    - *Sensor:* Willpower (Directional Time).

2.  **Where** (The Locus Vector) ![](media/image11.png){width="0.20182305336832895in" height="0.24431211723534557in"} **The Physical Plane**

    - *Target:* Context, Coordinates, Territory.

    - *Sensor:* Locality (Distance).

3.  **What** (The Definition Vector) ![](media/image11.png){width="0.20182305336832895in" height="0.24431211723534557in"} **The Possible Plane**

    - *Target:* Probability, Risk, Substance.

    - *Sensor:* Verification (Faith/Risk).

4.  **Why** (The Drive Vector) ![](media/image11.png){width="0.20182305336832895in" height="0.24431211723534557in"} **The Lyrical Plane**

    - *Target:* Resonance, Meaning, Emotional Drive.

    - *Sensor:* Resonance (Non-Euclidean Time).

5.  **How** (The Method Vector) ![](media/image11.png){width="0.20182305336832895in" height="0.24431211723534557in"} **The Logical Plane**

    - *Target:* Validity, Consistency, Process.

    - *Sensor:* Computation (Process Time).

6.  **Cause** (The Origin Vector) ![](media/image11.png){width="0.20182305336832895in" height="0.24431211723534557in"} **The Historical Plane**

    - *Target:* Lineage, Memory, Causal Chain.

    - *Sensor:* Sequence (Linear Time).

7.  **Effect** (The Impact Vector) ![](media/image11.png){width="0.20182305336832895in" height="0.24431211723534557in"} **The Emotive Plane**

    - *Target:* Strain, Harmony, Global Trust.

    - *Sensor:* Strain (Energetic Time).

## Step 1: ![](media/image1.png){width="0.23193350831146106in" height="0.2530183727034121in"} - The Gut Check (The Prior State)
