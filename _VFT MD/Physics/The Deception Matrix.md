## **Part 2: The Tactical Grid (The Senses x Uses)**

Once you know the **Plane** (e.g., They are lying about *History/Cause*), you locate the specific **Tactic** by cross-referencing the **Sense** they are appealing to and the **Use** (Intent) of the lie.

*How is the lie entering your brain?*

1. **SIGHT:** Visuals, symbols, appearances, aesthetics.  
2. **HEARING:** Tone, volume, rhythm, speed, music.  
3. **TOUCH:** Intimacy, safety, friction, boundaries, texture.  
4. **SMELL:** Intuition, atmosphere, disgust, pheromones.  
5. **TASTE:** Palatability, bitterness, sweetness, digestion.  
6. **BALANCE:** Fairness, equity, stability, rules of the game.  
7. **PROPRIOCEPTION:** Orientation, distance, location, self-position.

1. **SURVIVAL:** Triggering fear to force compliance. (The Threat).  
2. **DOMINATION:** Triggering awe/submission to force obedience. (The Power).  
3. **EXPRESSION:** Triggering sentiment to force connection. (The Performance).  
4. **TRANSCENDENCE:** Triggering faith to force blindness. (The Cult).  
5. **ACQUISITION:** Triggering greed to force action. (The Grift).

## **Part 3: Rapid Diagnostic Protocol (The "Bullshit" Scanner)**

When encountering a suspicious narrative, run this 3-step loop:

### **Step 1: Isolate the "Hum" (The Emotive Check)**

*Does this information make me feel an intense, unearned emotion?*

* If **YES**, proceed to Step 2\.  
* *Common Hums:* Panic, Rage, Smug Superiority, Hopelessness, Euphoria.

### **Step 2: Identify the Missing Variable (The Plane Check)**

*Which of the 7 Meta-Questions is being suppressed or distorted?*

* **Is the Speaker hidden?** \-\> Check Plane 1 (Who).  
* **Is the Reason vague?** \-\> Check Plane 2 (Why).  
* **Is the History altered?** \-\> Check Plane 3 (Cause).  
* **Is the Promise impossible?** \-\> Check Plane 4 (What).  
* **Is the Location shifted?** \-\> Check Plane 5 (Where).  
* **Is the Process hidden?** \-\> Check Plane 6 (How).  
* **Is the Result pure feeling?** \-\> Check Plane 7 (Effect).

### **Step 3: Name the Tactic (The Matrix Lock)**

*Combine the Sense \+ Use to name the lie.*

* *Example:* A politician uses a **Graphic Image (Sight)** of a past war to create **Fear (Survival)** in order to justify a new law.  
  * **Diagnosis:** Plane 3 (Cause) \-\> Sight x Survival \= **"The Bloody Shirt" (3.1.1)**.  
  * **Counter-Measure:** "You are using a scar from the past to bypass logic in the present. Show me the current threat data, not the old wound."

## **Part 4: The Universal Counter-Measures**

**1\. The "Specifics" Razor**

* **The Lie:** Vague, Emotional, Infinite.  
* **The Counter:** "Be specific. Name the person. Name the location. Name the cost. Name the mechanic."  
* **Why it works:** Lies cannot survive specificity.

**2\. The "Qui Crapit" (Who Profits?)**

* **The Lie:** Altruistic, Urgent, Necessary.  
* **The Counter:** "If I believe this, who gets my money/vote/power?"  
* **Why it works:** It reveals the Acquisition vector hiding behind the Transcendence mask.

**3\. The "Slow Down"**

* **The Lie:** Urgent, Fast, Now or Never.  
* **The Counter:** "I will wait 24 hours before reacting."  
* **Why it works:** Emotional lies have a short half-life. They decay into absurdity if given time.

**4\. The "Mechanism" Check**

* **The Lie:** Magic, Inevitable, Complex.  
* **The Counter:** "Explain the mechanism step-by-step like I am 10 years old."  
* **Why it works:** If they can't explain the simple mechanics, the complex system is a fraud.
