# The 9 States of Belief: A VFT Matrix

This document outlines the nine fundamental psychological and social states that emerge from the interaction between an individual's internal judgment of an idea and their perception of the collective social judgment.

The matrix is defined by two axes:

- **Y-Axis (Personal Answer):** The individual's internal conclusion about an idea (Truth = 1, Lie \< 1, Insult \> 1).

- **X-Axis (Projected Social Answer):** The individual's prediction of how society will judge the same idea (Truth = 1, Lie \< 1, Insult \> 1).
