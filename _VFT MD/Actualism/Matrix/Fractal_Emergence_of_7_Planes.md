# The Fractal Emergence of the 7 Planes

*Derived from VFT Research Logs (Claude Inf Navigation)*

## The Infinite 2D Plane as Origin
The 7 Planes of Reality are not pre-existing containers but distinct **emergent properties** that arise from exploring variants on an infinite 2D plane.

The model posits that as you explore from any single point (Unity/1.0) on a 2D surface, you create more variables and sub-fractal layers. This process is described as "mining numbers" — the act of defining coordinates where none previously existed.

## The Mechanism of Directional Unlocking
Movement creates dimensions through a strict dependency chain:

1.  **Left/Right Exploration (X-Axis):**
    *   Initial movement mines the X-axis numbers.
    *   This action **unlocks** the potential for Up/Down movement.

2.  **Up/Down Exploration (Y-Axis):**
    *   Movement along the Y-axis unlocks further Left/Right potential on **sub-layers**.
    *   This essentially creates a fractal quantization structure.

## The 4 Directions and Sub-Layers
In effect, each layer possesses its own set of **4 directions** (Left, Right, Up, Down) plus a **Backwards and Forwards** orientation within the layers themselves.

*   **Fractal Quantization:** The bounds of movement are determined by this structure.
*   **Energy Requirement:** Necessarily, one needs more energy to move than the rate at which numbers are "mined."
*   **6-Directional Reality:** In every "pixel" of reality, there exists the ability to move in one of 6 directions relative to a single point (or 4 spatial directions with 2 modes).

## Energy and The Creation of Numbers
If you add onto your coordinates in an infinite plane, you are discovering or creating more numbers. This creation makes the new number a "target" relative to the origin.

*   Any number relative to itself is 1.
*   Movement requires energy because defining the path (mining the numbers) imposes a computational or energetic cost (The Cost of Being).
