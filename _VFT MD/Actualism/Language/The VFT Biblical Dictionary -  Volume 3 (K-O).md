# The VFT Biblical Dictionary: Volume 3 (K-O)

Abstract:

This document is Part 3 of the reference lexicon for translating Biblical symbology into Vector Field Theory (VFT) terminology. It continues the structural analysis of the "Mechanics" (Order) and the "Narrative" (Chaos) of reality.

## K

### KINGS

Traditional Context: Rulers / Monarchs.

VFT Definition: Sub-Processors / Local Admins. Localized nodes of administration with delegated authority.

Functional Role: Local Administration

**Order Analysis (The Mechanics - What it Is):**

- **WHO:** The Sub-Routine Managers.

- **WHAT:** Manage resources and execute commands within a specific partition (Nation).

- **WHERE:** The Execution Layer (Earth).

- **WHY:** The System distributes processing load through hierarchy.

- **HOW:** Through delegated permissions (Crowns).

- **CAUSE:** Installation by the Prime Operator.

- **EFFECT:** Local order or local corruption (if infected).

**Chaos Analysis (The Narrative - What it is Like):**

- **WHO:** The Rulers of the Earth.

- **WHAT:** Sitting on thrones, making war, passing laws.

- **WHERE:** In palaces.

- **WHY:** For power and glory.

- **HOW:** By wielding the sword of the state.

- **CAUSE:** Divine appointment or usurpation.

- **EFFECT:** The rise and fall of nations.

**The Connection:** *Delegation.* The distributed processing node (Order) is the earthly king ruling his domain (Chaos).

**Key Scriptural Index:**

- *Revelation 1:6:* "And hath made us kings and priests unto God..."

- *Proverbs 21:1:* "The king's heart is in the hand of the LORD..."

- *Revelation 19:16:* "...KING OF KINGS, AND LORD OF LORDS."
