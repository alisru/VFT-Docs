## PHASE 1: THE STRUCTURAL SCAN (The Reality Check)

**Goal:** Before doing math, does this idea even exist on all planes of reality?

*Replaces "Multi-Lens Inquiry" with "7-Plane Alignment".*

### 1.1 The Axis Probe (The Gut Check)

- **υ-Axis (Morality):** Who specifically benefits? (All vs. Self)

- **ψ-Axis (Will):** Is it trying to *Create* (Proactive) or *Prevent* (Suppressive)?

### 1.2 The 7 Core Perspectives (The Multi-Lens)

*Mapping the Original Framework to the 7 Planes of Reality.*

**The Utilitarian (The Physical Plane Stress Test):** Focus on the distribution of outcomes.

"Who specifically benefits from this idea? Who is harmed or bears the cost?"

"Does it create new value for the collective, or merely transfer value from one group to another?"

"What is the long-term impact on the whole system vs. its individual parts?"

"Is the logic dependent on an 'other' that must be diminished?"

**The Strategic (The Conscious Plane Stress Test):** Focus on underlying function vs. stated purpose.

"What is the stated goal vs. the most likely outcome?"

"Who gains power, influence, or control if this is adopted?"

"Does this align with the known motivations of the originator?"

"What are the second and third-order effects?"

**The Systemic (The Logical Plane Stress Test):** Focus on stability and institutional integrity.

"Does this reinforce or undermine the foundational rules of the system?"

"Does it promote stability and coherence, or introduce chaos and ambiguity?"

"Does it increase or decrease trust in institutions?"

**The Devil's Advocate (The Possible Plane Stress Test):** Focus on flaws and catastrophic failure.

"What is the most compelling criticism of this idea?"

"In what ways could this fail catastrophically?"

"What are the consequences proponents are ignoring?"

**The Historian (The Historical Plane Stress Test):** Focus on origin and context.

"Where did this idea come from? Who tried it before?"

"Does it acknowledge its history, or does it pretend to be new?"

"Is it erasing the past to control the future?"

**The Empath (The Emotional Plane Stress Test):** Focus on human resonance.

"Does this rely on hope (Growth) or fear (Control) to motivate?"

"Is the emotion being manipulated to bypass logic?"

"What is the 'Gut Feel'—Expansion or Contraction?"

**The Poet (The Lyrical Plane Stress Test):** Focus on narrative coherence.

"Is the story beautiful and coherent, or ugly and bureaucratic?"

"Does the language flow, or is it full of jargon and obfuscation?"

"Is it a Song, or a Noise?"

## PHASE 2: THE MATHEMATICAL VERIFICATION (The Value)

**Goal:** Calculate the Vector Coordinates using the **Universal Price Equation**.

### 2.1 The Moral Coordinate (υ) \[Horizontal\]

*Source:* **The Universal Force Equation of Price**

*Axiom:* Value is the ratio of Local Want (Strong Force) to Global Need (Weak Force).

**The Full Equation:**

Price = \[ Your(Need/Want) ÷ Society(Need/Want) \] × \[ Scarcity ÷ Renewability \] × \[ Others(Need/Want) ÷ Society(Need/Want) \]

- **Term 1 (Buyer's Relativity):** \[Your Need / Society Need\]

  - Measures the "Strong Force" (Local Urgency).

- **Term 2 (Supply Relativity):** \[Scarcity / Renewability\]

  - Measures physical limitations.

- **Term 3 (External Relativity):** \[Others Need / Society Need\]

  - Measures the "External Cost" or "Opportunity Cost." Does your consumption deprive others of a critical need?

**Calculation of the Moral Vector (υ):**

**υ = Value_Created_for_Society (Weak Force) - Value_Extracted_for_Self (Strong Force)**

- *Note on Extraction:* If Term 3 is High (Your action deprives others), Value_Extracted spikes, driving υ into negative territory.

- *Result:* **+1.0 (Universal Good)** vs **-1.0 (Parasitic Extraction)**.

### 2.2 The Will Coordinate (ψ) \[Vertical\]

*Formula:* **ψ = Energy × Direction**

- **Active (+):** Building, Creating, Seeking.

- **Passive (-):** Ignoring, Complaining, Preventing.

### 2.3 The Secondary Calculations (The Fine Tuning)

*Additional vectors required for high-fidelity judgment.*

**A. The Radius of Benefit (Scope):**

*Formula:* **Scope = N_beneficiaries ÷ N_total_system**

- **Scope = 1.0:** Universal (+υ).

- **R = 1:** Truth (Solid).

**Goal:** The Idea is valid (Phase 1 & 2), but is the **Actor** compromised?

*This is the "Personal Layer" you identified. We only run this to detect Hypocrisy.*

### 3.1 The Subjective Bias Check

- **The Tribal Gap:** Does the Actor apply this rule to themselves (Level 1) or just to "The Other" (Level 3)?

- **The Hypocrisy Gap (ΔH):**

  - *Stated Ideal:* "We must lower emissions."

  - *Actual Action:* "I fly private."

  - *Calculation:* **ΔH = \|\| Vector_Ideal - Vector_Action \|\|**

  - *Verdict:* High ΔH. The Idea is valid, but the Actor is a **Hypocrite** (Low Wisdom).
