# The Expanded Triadic Model: Causal & Source Matrices

This document expands the 16-point "State Matrix" into a dynamic, causal flow chart. It adds the temporal dimensions of **Cause (Seed)** and **Effect (Fruit)** to each plane, and introduces the **Source Analysis Matrix** for evaluating the messenger.

## 1. The Expanded Virtue Matrix (The Flow of Truth)

This matrix maps the complete lifecycle of a Virtue, from its originating Seed (Cause) to its final Fruit (Effect) in the world.

### Definitions of the Causal Extensions (Virtue)

- **Theological Plane:**

  - **Seed (Humility):** The admission of ignorance is the necessary prerequisite for all higher learning and faith.

  - **Fruit (Wisdom):** The ultimate result of processed Hope and Faith is the ability to discern the divine perspective.

- **Experiential Plane:**

  - **Seed (Curiosity):** The active desire to know is the spark that drives all physical investigation.

  - **Fruit (Mastery):** The result of Prudence applied to Objectivity is the complete command over one's environment.

- **Social Plane:**

  - **Seed (Duty):** The recognition of obligation to others is the foundation of all community.

  - **Fruit (Peace):** The result of Justice and Fortitude is a stable, harmonious social order.

- **Synthesized Plane:**

  - **Seed (Love):** The will to connect and integrate is the fundamental drive of the "Why."

  - **Fruit (Freedom):** The ultimate result of Truth is liberation from the Lie and the constraints of Delusion.

## 2. The Expanded Vice Matrix (The Flow of Delusion)

This matrix maps the lifecycle of a Vice, from its originating Seed (Cause) to its final Fruit (Effect) in the world.

### Definitions of the Causal Extensions (Vice)

- **Theological Plane:**

  - **Seed (Pride):** The belief that one already knows, or is superior to truth, prevents all learning.

  - **Fruit (Madness):** The ultimate result of Nihilism and Despair is the complete fragmentation of the mind.

- **Experiential Plane:**

  - **Seed (Apathy):** The lack of desire to know leads to stagnation and willful ignorance.

  - **Fruit (Incompetence):** The result of Folly and Dogma is the total inability to navigate reality effectively.

- **Social Plane:**

  - **Seed (Entitlement):** The belief that one is owed something by others without contribution destroys community.

  - **Fruit (Conflict/War):** The result of Corruption and Cowardice is the breakdown of order into violence.

- **Synthesized Plane:**

  - **Seed (Fear):** The fear of truth or pain drives the creation of the comfortable Lie.

  - **Fruit (Slavery):** The ultimate result of Delusion is bondage to the Lie and the inability to act freely.

## 3. The Source Analysis Matrix (The Opposing Who)

This matrix evaluates the **Messenger** presenting the information. It calibrates the "Trust" variable in the interaction.

  ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
  **Variable**            **Virtuous Source (Trustworthy)**                                                                            **Vicious Source (Untrustworthy)**
  ----------------------- ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------ --------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
  **Knowledge**           **Competence:** Does the source *actually* know the truth? Have they done the work (Grounded Integration)?   **Ignorance:** Is the source speaking from Ungrounded Abstraction? Do they lack the requisite Mastery?

  **Assumption**          **Charity:** Does the source assume the best of you? Are they trying to help/teach?                          **Malice/Contempt:** Does the source assume you are foolish, evil, or a resource to be exploited?

  **Trust**               **Integrity:** Is the source's internal definition aligned with their external action? (Low Strain).         **Hypocrisy:** Is there a high strain between what they say and what they do? (High Strain).
  ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

### The Trust Algorithm

1.  **Check Knowledge:** If **Ignorance** is high, the information is likely **Folly** (even if well-intentioned).

2.  **Check Assumption:** If **Malice** is high, the information is likely **Deceit** (a weaponized Lie).

3.  **Check Integrity:** If **Hypocrisy** is high, the source is a **Fake Maximiser** or **Grifter**.

- **Result:** If all three are negative, the source is a **Minimiser Agent**.

- **Result:** If Knowledge is high but Assumption is negative, the source is a **Dragon** (Hoarder).

- **Result:** If Knowledge is low but Assumption is positive, the source is a **Fool** (Well-meaning but dangerous).
