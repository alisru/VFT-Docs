## **The Conceptual Framework**

To understand the "Why" of any vector in the Hegemony, we must understand the *depth* of its processing. Reality is not experienced directly; it is filtered through a stack of existence.

- **n1: The Plane (The Context):** *Where* is the energy existing?

- **n2: The Sense (The Input):** *How* is the energy perceived?

- **n3: The Use (The Output):** *Why* is the energy processed? (Defined as a choice between Virtue and Vice, answering the Metaquestion).

## **n1: The 7 Planes of Reality**

**1. The Meta-Physical Plane (WHO)**

- **Definition:** The Realm of Will and Direction. The "Psochic" Vector.

- **Core Metric:** Directional Time.

**2. The Lyrical Plane (WHY)**

- **Definition:** The Realm of Meaning and Resonance. Connection by narrative rather than sequence.

- **Core Metric:** Non-Euclidean Time.

**3. The Historical Plane (CAUSE)**

- **Definition:** The Realm of Sequence and Causality. The log of "Afters" and "Befores."

- **Core Metric:** Linear Time.

**4. The Possible Plane (WHAT)**

- **Definition:** The Realm of Faith and Probability. Treating future probability as present certainty.

- **Core Metric:** Resolution Time.

**5. The Physical Plane (WHERE)**

- **Definition:** The Realm of Matter and Distance. The tangible cosmos.

- **Core Metric:** Euclidean Space.

**6. The Logical Plane (HOW)**

- **Definition:** The Realm of Count and Consistency. Operational laws and syntax.

- **Core Metric:** Computational Time.

**7. The Emotive Plane (EFFECT)**

- **Definition:** The Realm of Passion and Consequence. The felt impact of reality.

- **Core Metric:** Energetic Time (Strain).

## **n3: The 7 Teleological Uses (The Moral Polarity & Metaquestion)**

### **1. Survival (Exist)**   \leftrightarrow   **The Physical Plane (WHERE)**

- **The Mapping:** Survival is the drive to maintain Locality and Existence. It answers "Where am I?" by asserting "I am Here."

- **Function:** Maintenance, Safety, Homeostasis, The Anchor.

- **Virtue:** **Thriving** (Sustainable Growth).

- **Vice:** **Mere Survival** (Scarcity Mindset / Hoarding).

### **2. Domination (Rule)**   \leftrightarrow   **The Conscious Plane (WHO)**

- **The Mapping:** Domination is the assertion of the Observer over the Observed. It answers "Who is sovereign?"

- **Function:** Authority, Command, Identity Assertion.

- **Virtue:** **Sovereignty** (Self-Rule / Responsibility).

- **Vice:** **Tyranny** (Control over Others).

### **3. Expression (Create)**   \leftrightarrow   **The Lyrical Plane (WHY)**

- **The Mapping:** Expression is the manifestation of Purpose. It answers "Why does this exist?" by providing Meaning and Resonance.

- **Function:** Art, Narrative, Definition of Truth.

- **Virtue:** **Truth-Telling** (Revelation).

- **Vice:** **Delusion** (Deception / False Narrative).

### **4. Transcendence (Evolve)**   \leftrightarrow   **The Historical Plane (CAUSE)**

- **The Mapping:** Transcendence is the mastery of the Origin. It is the integration of the Cause to redeem the chain of events.

- **Function:** Evolution, Destiny, Connecting to the Source.

- **Virtue:** **Redemption** (Integrating the past to improve the future).

- **Vice:** **Revisionism** (Erasing or lying about the past).

### **5. Acquisition (Gather)**   \leftrightarrow   **The Possible Plane (WHAT)**

- **The Mapping:** Acquisition is the targeting of Potential. It answers "What do I want?" by collapsing the flux into a tangible object.

- **Function:** Ambition, Greed, Manifestation of the Possible.

- **Virtue:** **Stewardship** (Gathering to Build).

- **Vice:** **Greed** (Gathering to Consume).

### **6. Understanding (Know)**   \leftrightarrow   **The Logical Plane (HOW)**

- **The Mapping:** Understanding is the decoding of Mechanism. It answers "How does it work?" by mapping the syntax.

- **Function:** Analysis, Logic, Proof.

- **Virtue:** **Wisdom** (Applied Truth).

- **Vice:** **Sophistry** (Twisting Logic for gain).

### **7. Connection (Bond)**   \leftrightarrow   **The Emotive Plane (EFFECT)**

- **The Mapping:** Connection is the experience of Impact. It answers "What is the Effect?" through shared energy transfer.

- **Function:** Bonding, Love, Strain Sharing.

- **Virtue:** **Love/Unity** (Shared Resonances).

- **Vice:** **Parasitism** (Draining Energy).
