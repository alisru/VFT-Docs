## 0. The Operational Key

To navigate the 343 states of the Cognitive Universe, one must understand the Threefold Operation. A concept is not a point; it is a vector with three coordinates.

>   \text{State} = \text{Base (Context  %7D%20%5Ctimes%20%5Ctext%7BLens%20(Function)%7D%20%5Ctimes%20%5Ctext%7BTexture%20(Detail)%7D#0)

1.  **The Base (The Planet):** The primary domain of inquiry (e.g., **Saturn/WHY**). This sets the "Physics" of the reality we are analyzing.

2.  **The Lens (The Vector):** The question applied to the Base (e.g., **Sun/WHO**). This determines the "Archetype" that manifests.

3.  **The Texture (The Sub-Fractal):** The fine adjustment applied to the Archetype (e.g., **Moon/WHERE**). This determines the specific "manifestation" or "location" of that idea.
