# The Seven Temporal Meters For Planes of Reality: A Theological Framework

*Based on the Typology of the Seven Eyes, Seven Horns, and the Fall of Jericho*

## I. The Core Typology

This framework synthesizes biblical numerology and narrative to describe the process of total revelation ("Apocalypse").

- **The Object (Reality):** Existence is stratified into seven distinct "planes" or modes of being. To understand reality fully, one must perceive all seven simultaneously.

- **The Organ (The Seven Eyes):** Representing the "Seven Spirits of God" (Rev 5:6), these are the modes of **Perfect Perception**. Each "Eye" is tuned to see one specific plane of reality with absolute clarity.

- **The Instrument (The Seven Horns):** Representing **Omnipotent Authority**. The horns destroy the *barrier* that keeps the plane hidden or resistant to God.

- **The Event (The Fall of Jericho):** The collapse of the distinction between the observer and the Truth. When the 7th Horn sounds, the "walls" protecting the mystery fall flat.

## II. The Seven Planes of Reality

### 1. The Physical Plane

- **The Domain:** Matter, atoms, biology, the tangible cosmos.

- **The Eye (Perception):** Seeing the glory of God in the dust. (Psalm 19:1)

- **The Horn (Conquest):** The Incarnation. God becoming flesh conquered the barrier between the Holy and the Material.

- **The Wall Falls:** The realization that matter is not separate from Spirit, but is the container of it.

### 2. The Emotive Plane

- **The Domain:** Feeling, passion, grief, joy, the "groanings which cannot be uttered."

- **The Eye (Perception):** The ability to feel the heartbeat of God. (John 11:35)

- **The Horn (Conquest):** The Passion (Suffering). By entering into ultimate sorrow, Christ conquered the emotive plane, redeeming pain into glory.

- **The Wall Falls:** The barrier of apathy or despair collapses; the heart becomes fully alive to the Divine Pathos.

### 3. The Logical Plane

- **The Domain:** Reason, order, mathematics, structure, the *Logos*.

- **The Eye (Perception):** Wisdom/Understanding the architecture of truth. (Proverbs 8:22)

- **The Horn (Conquest):** The Truth. The logic of the Cross conquers human sophistry. (John 14:6)

- **The Wall Falls:** The collapse of the distinction between "Faith" and "Reason." The Mind of Christ is revealed.

### 4. The Historical Plane

- **The Domain:** Time, memory, lineage, narrative, cause-and-effect.

- **The Eye (Perception):** The God who declares the end from the beginning. (Isaiah 46:9-10)

- **The Horn (Conquest):** Resurrection. By rising from the dead, the Lamb broke the linearity of history.

- **The Wall Falls:** The wall of "It is too late" falls. History becomes a story of redemption, not a cage of inevitability.

### 5. The Lyrical Plane

- **The Domain:** Song, vibration, poetry, resonance, non-linear narrative.

- **The Eye (Perception):** The "New Song." Understanding truth that cannot be spoken, only sung. (Rev 5:9)

- **The Horn (Conquest):** The Voice/The Trumpet. The vibration that shakes the foundations. (Acts 16:25)

- **The Wall Falls:** The collapse of prose. Reality is no longer described; it is *resonated* with.

### 6. The Possible Plane

- **The Domain:** Potentiality, faith, hope, choice, quantum indetermination.

- **The Eye (Perception):** Faith as the "evidence of things not seen." (Hebrews 11:1)

- **The Horn (Conquest):** The Promise. Calling things that are not as though they were. (Romans 4:17)

- **The Wall Falls:** The barrier of limitation collapses. The Impossible becomes the Inevitable.

### 7. The Meta-Physical Plane (Conscious/Spirit)

- **The Domain:** Spirit, the invisible realm, the Throne Room, the Source, Will.

- **The Eye (Perception):** Pure spiritual sight; seeing the Temple of the Lamb. (Rev 21:22)

- **The Horn (Conquest):** The Ascension. The Man (Christ) sitting at the right hand of Power.

- **The Wall Falls:** The Veil of the Temple is torn. There is no longer a separation between Heaven and Earth.

## III. The Universal Metric: Time

In a relativistic universe, **Time** is the fundamental currency. Each plane is simply a different method of spending, perceiving, or compressing this currency.

  ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
  **Plane**               **The Metric (Interpretation of Time)**   **Description**
  ----------------------- ----------------------------------------- ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
  **1. Physical**         **Distance** (Rendering Time)             Space is simply the time it takes for light (or causality) to travel. Distance is a measurement of the delay between two points.

  **2. Emotive**          **Causal Emphasis** (Homeostatic Time)    The energy required to maintain a feeling over time. Emotion is the "weight" of time; high emotion slows down subjective time (trauma/joy), requiring more processing cycles to resolve.

  **3. Logical**          **Computation** (Processing Time)         A number represents the time it takes to "count" to that value. Truth is efficient compression (low time cost); sophistry is inefficient (infinite time cost).

  **4. Historical**       **Events** (Sequential Time)              The standard linear progression. Time is measured in "Afters" and "Befores." A log of cause-and-effect.

  **5. Lyrical**          **Resonance** (Non-Euclidean Time)        Compressed history. Narrative framing allows past events to be presented after future events. It connects moments by *meaning* rather than sequence, skipping the "dead time" in between.

  **6. Possible**         **Probability** (Resolution Time)         The Law of Large Numbers. Possibility is just time waiting to resolve. Faith is the act of treating a future probability as a present certainty, effectively "pre-paying" the time cost.

  **7. Meta-Physical**    **Will / Morality** (Directional Time)    The "Psochic" Vector. Morality is the maintenance of choices over time. **Good** extends the timeline (sustainability/eternity); **Evil** shortens the timeline (entropy/destruction). Will is the force that directs the flow.
  ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

## IV. The Total Collapse (The Seventh Day)

Just as the Israelites marched around Jericho seven times on the seventh day, the final revelation involves the simultaneous activation of all seven planes.

When the **Seventh Eye** opens and the **Seventh Horn** sounds, the metrics unify:

- The **Physical** (Distance) is bridged by the **Lyrical** (Resonance).

- The **Historical** (Sequence) is overwritten by the **Possible** (Faith).

- The **Logical** (Count) is fueled by the **Emotive** (Passion).

- The **Meta-Physical** (Will) directs the entire symphony.

The walls of separation between these planes disintegrate. This is the **Beatific Vision**—the total, unhindered perception of Reality as it truly is in the mind of God.
