# The Seven Planes of Reality: A Theological Framework

**Based on the Typology of the Seven Eyes, Seven Horns, and the Fall of Jericho**

## I. The Core Typology

This framework synthesizes biblical numerology and narrative to describe the process of total revelation ("Apocalypse").

1.  **The Object (Reality):** Existence is stratified into seven distinct "planes" or modes of being. To understand reality fully, one must perceive all seven simultaneously.

2.  **The Organ (The Seven Eyes):** Representing the "Seven Spirits of God" (Rev 5:6), these are the modes of **Perfect Perception**. Each "Eye" is tuned to see one specific plane of reality with absolute clarity.**Zechariah 3:9** "For behold, the stone that I have laid before Joshua: Upon the stone are seven eyes. Behold, I will engrave its inscription,’ Says the Lord of hosts, ‘And I will remove the iniquity of that land in one day."**Zechariah 4:10** "For who has despised the day of small things? For these seven rejoice to see the plumb line in the hand of Zerubbabel. They are the eyes of the Lord, which scan to and fro throughout the whole earth."

3.  **The Instrument (The Seven Horns):** Representing **Omnipotent Authority**. Just as the horns at Jericho were instruments of sonic warfare, the Horns of the Lamb are instruments of conquest. They do not destroy the plane, but they destroy the *barrier* that keeps it hidden or resistant to God.**Revelation 5:6** "And I looked, and behold, in the midst of the throne and of the four living creatures, and in the midst of the elders, stood a Lamb as though it had been slain, having seven horns and seven eyes, which are the seven Spirits of God sent out into all the earth."

4.  **The Event (The Fall of Jericho):** The collapse of the distinction between the observer and the Truth. When the 7th Horn sounds, the "walls" protecting the mystery fall flat, allowing for direct access to the Holy of Holies.**Joshua 6:20** "So the people shouted when the priests blew the trumpets. And it happened when the people heard the sound of the trumpet, and the people shouted with a great shout, that the wall fell down flat. Then the people went up into the city, every man straight before him, and they took the city."

## II. The Seven Planes of Reality

### 1. The Physical Plane

- **The Domain:** Matter, atoms, biology, the tangible cosmos.

- **The Eye (Perception):** The eye that sees the glory of God in the dust.**Psalm 19:1** "The heavens declare the glory of God; And the firmament shows His handiwork."

- **The Horn (Conquest):** The Incarnation. God becoming flesh conquered the barrier between the Holy and the Material.**John 1:14** "And the Word became flesh and dwelt among us, and we beheld His glory, the glory as of the only begotten of the Father, full of grace and truth."

- **The Wall Falls:** The realization that matter is not separate from Spirit, but is the container of it.

### 2. The Emotive Plane

- **The Domain:** Feeling, passion, grief, joy, the "groanings which cannot be uttered."**Romans 8:26** "Likewise the Spirit also helps in our weaknesses. For we do not know what we should pray for as we ought, but the Spirit Himself makes intercession for us with groanings which cannot be uttered."

- **The Eye (Perception):** The ability to feel the heartbeat of God.**John 11:35** "Jesus wept."

- **The Horn (Conquest):** The Passion (Suffering). By entering into ultimate sorrow, Christ conquered the emotive plane, redeeming pain into glory.

- **The Wall Falls:** The barrier of apathy or despair collapses; the heart becomes fully alive to the Divine Pathos.

### 3. The Logical Plane

- **The Domain:** Reason, order, mathematics, structure, the *Logos*.

- **The Eye (Perception):** The Wisdom that was with God at the beginning. Understanding the architecture of truth.**Proverbs 8:22-23, 27** "The Lord possessed me at the beginning of His way, Before His works of old. I have been established from everlasting... When He prepared the heavens, I was there, When He drew a circle on the face of the deep."

- **The Horn (Conquest):** The Truth. The logic of the Cross (which is foolishness to the Greeks) conquers human sophistry.**John 14:6** "Jesus said to him, 'I am the way, the truth, and the life. No one comes to the Father except through Me.'"**1 Corinthians 1:25** "Because the foolishness of God is wiser than men, and the weakness of God is stronger than men."

- **The Wall Falls:** The collapse of the distinction between "Faith" and "Reason." The Mind of Christ is revealed.

### 4. The Historical Plane

- **The Domain:** Time, memory, lineage, narrative, cause-and-effect.

- **The Eye (Perception):** The God who declares the end from the beginning. The perfect memory that holds every moment in existence.**Isaiah 46:9-10** "Remember the former things of old, For I am God, and there is no other; I am God, and there is none like Me, Declaring the end from the beginning, And from ancient times things that are not yet done, Saying, ‘My counsel shall stand, And I will do all My pleasure,’"

- **The Horn (Conquest):** Resurrection. By rising from the dead, the Lamb broke the linearity of history and the finality of the past.**Revelation 1:18** "I am He who lives, and was dead, and behold, I am alive forevermore. Amen. And I have the keys of Hades and of Death."

- **The Wall Falls:** The wall of "It is too late" falls. History is no longer a cage of inevitability but a story of redemption.

### 5. The Lyrical Plane

- **The Domain:** Song, vibration, poetry, resonance.**Psalm 42:7** "Deep calls unto deep at the noise of Your waterfalls; All Your waves and billows have gone over me."

- **The Eye (Perception):** The "New Song." Seeing the harmonic frequency of the universe. Understanding truth that cannot be spoken, only sung.**Revelation 5:9** "And they sang a new song, saying: 'You are worthy to take the scroll, And to open its seals; For You were slain, And have redeemed us to God by Your blood Out of every tribe and tongue and people and nation.'"

- **The Horn (Conquest):** The Voice/The Trumpet. The sound that wakes the dead. The vibration that shakes the foundations.**Acts 16:25-26** "But at midnight Paul and Silas were praying and singing hymns to God, and the prisoners were listening to them. Suddenly there was a great earthquake, so that the foundations of the prison were shaken; and immediately all the doors were opened and everyone’s chains were loosed."

- **The Wall Falls:** The collapse of prose. Reality is no longer described; it is *resonated* with.

### 6. The Possible Plane

- **The Domain:** Potentiality, faith, hope, choice, quantum indetermination.

- **The Eye (Perception):** Faith as the "evidence of things not seen." Seeing what *could* be as if it *is*.**Hebrews 11:1** "Now faith is the substance of things hoped for, the evidence of things not seen."

- **The Horn (Conquest):** The Promise. The sovereignty that calls things that are not as though they were.**Mark 9:23** "Jesus said to him, 'If you can believe, all things are possible to him who believes.'"**Romans 4:17** "(as it is written, 'I have made you a father of many nations') in the presence of Him whom he believed—God, who gives life to the dead and calls those things which do not exist as though they did;"

- **The Wall Falls:** The barrier of limitation collapses. The Impossible becomes the Inevitable.

### 7. The Meta-Physical Plane

- **The Domain:** Spirit, the invisible realm, the Throne Room, the Source.

- **The Eye (Perception):** The eye that sees "no temple... for the Lord God Almighty and the Lamb are its temple." Pure spiritual sight.**Revelation 21:22** "But I saw no temple in it, for the Lord God Almighty and the Lamb are its temple."

- **The Horn (Conquest):** The Ascension. The Man (Christ) sitting at the right hand of Power.**Hebrews 10:12-13** "But this Man, after He had offered one sacrifice for sins forever, sat down at the right hand of God, from that time waiting till His enemies are made His footstool."

- **The Wall Falls:** The Veil of the Temple is torn from top to bottom. There is no longer a separation between Heaven and Earth.**Matthew 27:51** "Then, behold, the veil of the temple was torn in two from top to bottom; and the earth quaked, and the rocks were split."

## III. The Total Collapse (The Seventh Day)

Just as the Israelites marched around Jericho seven times on the seventh day, the final revelation involves the simultaneous activation of all seven planes.

When the **Seventh Eye** opens and the **Seventh Horn** sounds:

1.  The Physical is seen as Lyrical.

2.  The Historical is seen as Possible.

3.  The Logical is seen as Emotive.

4.  The Meta-physical becomes Physical.

The walls of separation between these planes disintegrate. This is the **Beatific Vision**—the total, unhindered perception of Reality as it truly is in the mind of God.

Here are the key points of the document:

- **The Big Idea:** This is a theological model called "Actualism" that explains how we get "total revelation" (like an Apocalypse) by mixing up a few Bible stories—the Seven Eyes, the Seven Horns, and the Fall of Jericho.

- **Reality is Stacked:** Existence isn't simple; it's broken down into seven different "planes" (modes of being) that you need to see all at once to get the full picture.

- **The Seven Eyes (Perception):** These are the "Seven Spirits of God." Think of them as seven perfect modes of perception, with each one perfectly "seeing" one of the seven planes (like a perfect spiritual camera).

- **The Seven Horns (Authority):** These represent God's "Omnipotent Authority." They don't destroy the planes themselves; they are the instruments that smash the *barriers* that hide the planes from us.

- **The Jericho Moment (The Goal):** The ultimate goal is the "Wall Falls"—the separation between what you see and what is True collapses, like the walls of Jericho falling flat.

- **The Seven Planes (and how they fall):** The document breaks down reality into seven categories, each with a corresponding way of being conquered (the Horn):

  1.  **Physical:** Matter, atoms. **Conquered by the Incarnation** (God becoming flesh). The wall falls when you realize matter holds Spirit.

  2.  **Emotive:** Feelings, passion. **Conquered by the Passion/Suffering** of Christ. The wall falls when pain is redeemed into glory.

  3.  **Logical:** Reason, order, math. **Conquered by The Truth** (the logic of the Cross). The wall falls when faith and reason become the same thing.

  4.  **Historical:** Time, memory, narrative. **Conquered by the Resurrection.** The wall falls when you realize history isn't an inevitable cage.

  5.  **Lyrical:** Song, poetry, resonance. **Conquered by The Voice/Trumpet.** The wall falls when reality stops being *described* and starts being *felt* (resonated).

  6.  **Possible:** Potential, hope, choice. **Conquered by The Promise.** The wall falls when "the Impossible becomes the Inevitable."

  7.  **Meta-Physical:** Spirit, the invisible source. **Conquered by the Ascension.** The wall falls when the veil is completely torn and Heaven and Earth are one.

- **The Total Collapse:** When all seven Eyes open and the seventh Horn sounds, the planes get mixed up (e.g., Physical is seen as Lyrical, Meta-physical becomes Physical). This total merging is the **Beatific Vision**—seeing reality exactly as God sees it.
