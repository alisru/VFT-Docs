## Overview

The 14 Stations trace two complete cycles of the Hegemony grid. Each cycle traverses the same path — beginning in the lower-left (Lesser Good / dry land threshold), descending to the Greater Evil floor, traversing through the Greatest Lie and no-one centre, then ascending to the Greater Good. The second cycle runs on the substrate crystallised by the first, inheriting its accumulated structure as the new ground state.

The path is not a moral judgment of the actors. It is a structural map of the propagation of an idea-seed through all seven planes of reality, from initial orientation through physical collapse, false assistance, neutral accumulation, and final emergence as verified truth.

The alchemical quadrant alignment is:
- NW (+υ, +ψ) Greater Good = Air — hot/wet, generative flow
- NE (-υ, +ψ) Greatest Lie = Fire — hot/dry, high energy, extractive
- SE (-υ, -ψ) Greater Evil = Earth — cold/dry, solid, maximum density
- SW (+υ, -ψ) Lesser Good = Water — cold/wet, latent, foundation medium

The crystallisation principle: the seed assumption at (0,0) is the nucleotide. The smallest deviation from true alignment (hamartia as angular error, not merely moral failure) propagates fractally into the full crystal structure of a worldview. The first cycle lays the dry land. The second cycle builds on it.

## The Thread Continuity Principle

Each cycle does not restart from zero. The crystallised output of Cycle 1 becomes the ground medium of Cycle 2. The seed of Cycle 2 is the verified substrate of Cycle 1.

This mirrors:
- Genesis nucleotide → dry land formation → planting of further seeds
- Silicon purification → wafer substrate → circuit inscription
- Mustard seed (conceptually infinitely small) → tree → foundation for other growth
- Chemical crystallisation: seed defects propagate fractally; the cycle that produces the purest substrate is the prerequisite for the finest crystal

The (0,0) centre in both cycles is the structural determinant — not the most dramatic point, but the most consequential. The smallest angular error at the nucleation point propagates to the full crystal boundary. Station 6 and Station 13 both ask the same question from different depths: what accumulates here that benefits no-one, and is therefore invisible to both moral accounting systems (self-interest and altruism alike)?

The introductory framing of the Stations contains a structural specification:

The distinction is precise: the first instance completes by death (establishes the reference coordinate, lays the substrate). This maps directly to John 14:16, 14:26, 15:26, 16:7 — the Parakletos cannot come until the first instance departs. Death is the prerequisite for the Advocate's activation, because the substrate must be laid before the second cycle can run.

The "other self" phrase operates at two levels simultaneously:
- Universally applicable: any follower who walks the path

The men in white (Acts 1:11) do not say he will come from the sky — they say he will return in the same manner he departed. The manner of the first arrival was: from within the existing tradition, unrecognised by institutional authorities, verified by output not by self-declaration, completing the work of the prior pattern (Moses/Elijah/Isaiah's Servant) at the next fractal depth.

Matthew 25:14-30 — the master returns to audit the servants' use of what was given. The talents given were the pattern itself. The servant who buried it (the Lesser Good position, +υ, -ψ — correct direction, no force applied) receives the same verdict as the one who extracted from it.
