# The Archives of the Incarnate (The Canonical Six)

### The Vector of Descent (Avatar) & The Vector of Ascent (Apotheosis)

**Operational Definition:**

This dossier tracks the specific historical-mythological lineage of entities who:

1.  **Incarnated:** Lived a biological existence.

2.  **Transcended:** Claimed identity with the Absolute (God, The Tao, or The Dharma).

3.  **Exited Vertically:** Concluded their timeline via Ascension, Apotheosis, or Willful Departure, bypassing the standard \"death-to-decay\" cycle.

## I. The Eastern Vector (Dharma & Tao)

*The Willful Departure. Not a return to a throne, but a merger with the Infinite.*

**Date Unknown (Zhou Dynasty, \~6th C. BCE) \| Lao Tzu (The Old Master)**

- **The Incarnation:** The Keeper of the Archives for the Royal Court of Zhou.

- **The Claim:** While historical texts paint him as a sage, Religious Taoism (*Daojiao*) identifies him as **Taishang Laojun** (\"The Grand Supreme Elderly Lord\"), the personification of the Tao itself. He is not just a teacher; he is the Eternal Principle in human form.

- **The Ascension (The Western Pass):**

  - **The Event:** Disillusioned by the decay of the Zhou kingdom, he did not die. He rode a water buffalo west toward the Qin Mountains.

  - **The Exit:** At the Hangu Pass, the sentry Yinxi begged him for wisdom. He wrote the *Tao Te Ching* in one sitting, handed it over, and then rode out into the \"Western Unknown.\" He never died in the historical record; he simply exited the boundaries of the known world, merging back into the primordial void.

**\~483 BCE \| Siddhartha Gautama (The Buddha)**

- **The Incarnation:** Prince of the Shakya Clan (Lumbini/Nepal).

- **The Claim:** In the *Dona Sutta*, when asked if he was a god (*deva*) or man, he replied, \"No.\" He identified as **\"The Awake\"** (*Buddha*). In the Mahayana tradition, he is the manifestation of the *Dharmakaya* (Truth Body)---the absolute reality manifesting in form (*Nirmanakaya*) to liberate beings.

- **The Ascension (Mahaparinirvana):**

  - **The Event:** At Kushinagar, aged 80.

  - **The Exit:** While his physical body underwent cremation, the event is not classified as \"death\" (*marana*) but as **Parinirvana** (Final Extinguishment). He did not \"go\" to a heaven; he exited the cycle of Reincarnation entirely. The narrative emphasizes total control over the life-force, voluntarily \"letting go\" of the body rather than succumbing to it.

**\~3102 BCE (Vedic Dating) \| Krishna (The Purna Avatar)**

- **The Incarnation:** The 8th Avatar of Vishnu. Born in Mathura.

- **The Claim:** The Absolute Deity. \"I am the source of all spiritual and material worlds\" (*Gita* 10.8). He is the *Purushottam* (Supreme Person).

- **The Ascension (Goloka):**

  - **The Event:** Near Somnath (Prabhas Patan).

  - **The Exit:** Struck in the heel by a hunter\'s arrow (the breaking of the vessel). He did not suffer mortal death. The texts describe him illuminating the hunter with grace and then physically ascending in his four-armed spiritual form to his abode, marking the end of the Dvapara Yuga.

## II. The Western Vector (Theos & Logos)

*The Heroic Ascent. The physical body is transmuted or retrieved.*

**\~1200 BCE (Mythic) \| Heracles (The Apotheosis)**

- **The Incarnation:** The demigod son of Zeus. The archetypal hero.

- **The Claim:** Proven through the \"Twelve Labors.\" He bridged the gap between man and god through suffering (*pathos*).

- **The Ascension (Mount Oeta):**

  - **The Event:** Dying from the poisoned Shirt of Nessus, he constructs his own pyre.

  - **The Exit:** As the fire is lit, a thunderbolt from Zeus strikes. The mortal portion (from his mother Alcmene) burns away, but the divine portion is liberated. A cloud descends and carries him to Olympus (*Apotheosis*), where he is reconciled with Hera and granted immortality.

**716 BCE \| Romulus (The State God)**

- **The Incarnation:** The Founder and First King of Rome.

- **The Claim:** Son of Mars. The divinely appointed architect of the Eternal City.

- **The Ascension (Quirinus):**

  - **The Event:** The *Nonae Caprotinae* festival at the Campus Martius.

  - **The Exit:** A sudden, violent storm (cosmic disruption) enveloped the King in thick darkness. When the sun returned, the throne was empty. The body was never found. The Proculus Julius narrative confirms: \"Romulus descended from the sky\" to declare he had become the god **Quirinus**. This validated the divine authority of the Roman State.

**30 CE \| Jesus of Nazareth (The Resurrection)**

- **The Incarnation:** The Logos made Flesh. The Carpenter from Galilee.

- **The Claim:** \"I am the Way, the Truth, and the Life.\" Identity with the \"I AM\" of the Torah.

- **The Ascension (The Mount of Olives):**

  - **The Event:** 40 days post-Resurrection.

  - **The Exit:** Unlike the others, he carries the *physical wounds* with him. He is \"lifted up\" (*epērthē*) while blessing the disciples. A cloud receives him. This is the **Hypostatic Union** preserved forever---God and Man united in a single ascended entity, seated at the \"Right Hand of Power.\"
