# The Digital Theology Stack: A Unified Architecture

## Layer 1: The Linguistic Namespace (The Nature of God)

### The Concept

Initially modeled as a C++ namespace, this layer evolved into a **Linguistic/Semantic Framework**. A namespace prevents ambiguity by providing a shared scope for distinct identifiers.

- **The Namespace (God)**: Represents the **Ousia** (Essence/Substance). It is the single reality that encompasses the whole. It is the context or shared definition that makes the Father, Son, and Spirit "God" rather than "Human" or "Angel."

- **The Identifiers (Father, Son, Spirit)**: These represent the **Hypostases** (Persons). They are distinct entities defined within that scope.

### The Theological Mapping

1.  **Unity of Scope (Consubstantiality)**: You cannot access the Persons without acknowledging they are of God. They exist within the same definition of reality.

2.  **Distinctness of Persons (Hypostasis)**: God::Father is functionally distinct from God::Son. The Father does not die on the cross; the Son does.

3.  **Properties vs. Attributes**:

    - **Attributes** (Omniscience, Love) are shared by the Namespace.

    - **Properties** (Unbegotten, Begotten, Proceeding) are unique to the Identifier.

### The Refinement: Semantics over Syntax

To avoid **Tritheism** (Three separate files) or **Nominalism** (God as just a label), we view the Namespace as the **Grammar of Divinity**. The "God" namespace is the shared context that gives meaning to the distinct properties. The Persons are distinct identities that only make sense within that context—relational predicates that cannot be swapped.

## Layer 2: Actus Purus (Functional Programming)

### The Concept

God is best described not by Object-Oriented Programming (which separates State from Behavior), but by **Functional Programming** (where Definition is Application).

- **The User's Insight**: "The functions are both the definitions and the applications; you cannot have one without the other."

- **The Theological Doctrine**: **Actus Purus** (Pure Actuality). God has no "dormant potential." He is always fully executing His nature.

### The Paradigm Shift

- **OOP Mistake**: Objects have state waiting to be used. (God *has* love).

- **FP Reality**: In pure functional calculus (Lambda Calculus), a function is defined entirely by its mapping. x -\> x + 1. The definition *is* the application.

  - **Father**: Eternally "applying" Source-hood (Generating the Son).

  - **Son**: Eternally "applying" Filiation (Being Generated).

  - **Spirit**: Eternally "applying" Procession.

**Conclusion**: God is **Immutable Code**. There are no "side effects" in the Immanent Trinity; it is a closed loop of perfect love and recursive glorification.
