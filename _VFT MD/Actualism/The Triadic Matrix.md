# **The Triadic Matrix: The 2 Planes of the 32 Isms**

## **1. The Coordinate System**

The map is a geometric field spanning from **+2.0 to -2.0** on both axes.

- **Axis** ![](media/image1.png){width="0.1385094050743657in" height="0.25570975503062116in"} **(Will):** The Vertical Axis. Spans **+2.0 to -2.0** in increments of **1.0**.

  - **Row 1 (+1.5):** The Synthesized Horizon (Range: +2.0 to +1.0)

  - **Row 2 (+0.5):** The Social Horizon (Range: +1.0 to 0.0)

  - **Row 3 (-0.5):** The Experiential Horizon (Range: 0.0 to -1.0)

  - **Row 4 (-1.5):** The Theological Horizon (Range: -1.0 to -2.0)

- **Axis** ![](media/image2.png){width="0.11621062992125984in" height="0.2535498687664042in"} **(Morality):** The Horizontal Axis. Spans **+2.0 to -2.0** in increments of **0.5**.

  - **Plane A (Realisms):** Positive values (+2.0 to 0.0).

  - **Plane B (Anti-Realisms):** Negative values (0.0 to -2.0).
