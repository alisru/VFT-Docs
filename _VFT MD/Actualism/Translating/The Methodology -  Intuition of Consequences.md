# The Methodology: Intuition of Consequences

## The Core Axiom

> "Act as if you cannot speak, only act, and judge accordingly.
>
> Act as if you cannot hear, only see, and judge the actions of words.
>
> Act as if you cannot see, only feel the effects of actions on the universe."

**The Principle:** This is a method of sensory deprivation designed to filter out "noise" (performance, charisma, promises) and isolate "signal" (causality). It shifts the definition of truth from *observation* to *impact*.

## The Definition of Truth

**"Truth is what IS, and IS over time."**

- **Description (The Lie):** Requires constant energy to maintain. It resists reality. If the narrator stops speaking, the lie vanishes.

- **Existence (The Truth):** Requires no energy. It has inertia. If you leave it for ten years and return, it is exactly where you left it.

- **Duration:** Truth is not an event; it is the integral of consequence over time. To define truth as "IS over time" is to admit you cannot judge truth in the split-second moment, but only by its durability.

## Hamilton's Razor

> **"Judge nothing by its description, only by its debris."**

- **Description** = What is seen/heard/spoken (The Senses).

- **Debris** = What is left behind, what physically changed, "what IS" (The Consequence).

## The Three Razors of Scrutiny

### 1. The Mute Button Razor (Separation of Source)

> **"Subtract the messenger. If the value collapses, it was never there; if it remains, it is real."**

- **Concept:** Value that requires a voice is performance. Value that survives silence is truth.

- **Application:** Ignore the sales pitch, the "vision," and the charisma. Look at the raw object or data in isolation. If it looks "stupid" without the explanation, it is stupid.

### 2. The Structural Stress Razor (Active Judgment)

> **"Silence the messenger to interrogate the message. True value survives the transition from passive hearing to active judging."**

- **Concept:** Treat information like a physical structure. When the builder (the speaker) leaves, push against the structure (logic/stress-test).

- **Application:** Don't just listen; audit. If the plan falls apart the moment you stress-test it without the boss present to defend it, it was never a plan—it was a wish.

### 3. The Cleanup Razor (The Residue)

> **"Judge the room after the music stops."**

- **Concept:** Silence is the ultimate lie detector. The truth is the residue left behind after the noise fades.

- **Application:** The "Hype Cycle." Everyone talks about the new thing. Wait until they stop. Is the technology actually useful (residue), or was it just noise?
