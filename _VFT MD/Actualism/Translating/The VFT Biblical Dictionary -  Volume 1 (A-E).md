- **Traditional Context:** Beginning and End.

  - **EFFECT:** A complete, computable cycle.

  - **EFFECT:** A sense of completeness and resolution ("It is finished").

- **The Connection:** **Completeness.** A story (Chaos) only has meaning if it has a start and a finish; a process (Order) only executes if the loop is defined.
