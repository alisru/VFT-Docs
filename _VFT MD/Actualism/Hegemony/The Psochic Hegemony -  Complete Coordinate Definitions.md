# The Psochic Hegemony: Coordinate Definitions

## 1. The Axis Definition

- **Horizontal Axis (**\$\\upsilon\$ **/ Nu) - Morality**

  - **Left (+):** Universal Benefit (Selfless).

  - **Right (-):** Self Benefit (Selfish).

- **Vertical Axis (**\$\\psi\$ **/ Psi) - Will**

  - **Top (+):** Proactive Will (Create/Do).

  - **Bottom (-):** Passive Will (Suppress/Allow).

## 2. The Inner 0.5 Ring: The Perceptual Inversion

**Definition:** This is the realm of the **Ego**. At this proximity to the self (0.5), values are inverted based on "How it feels to Me" rather than "What it does for All."

### Top Right Quadrant (The Ambition Trap)

- **Coordinate:** (-0.5 υ, +0.5 ψ)

- **Objective Name:** The Greatest Lie (Selfish Action).

- **Subjective Label:** **P-GG (Subjective Greater Good)**.

- **The Inversion:** The Ego calls this "Good" because it feels like **Winning**.

  - *Perception:* Strength, Drive, Success, "The Hustle."

### Top Left Quadrant (The Service Trap)

- **Coordinate:** (+0.5 υ, +0.5 ψ)

- **Objective Name:** The Greater Good (Selfless Action).

- **Subjective Label:** **P-LE (Subjective Lesser Evil)**.

- **The Inversion:** The Ego calls this "Evil" (or a negative) because it feels like a **Burden**.

  - *Perception:* Naivety, Unpaid Labor, "Being a Doormat," Sacrifice.

### Bottom Left Quadrant (The Peace Trap)

- **Coordinate:** (+0.5 υ, -0.5 ψ)

- **Objective Name:** The Lesser Good (Universal Stability).

- **Subjective Label:** **P-GE (Subjective Greater Evil)**.

- **The Inversion:** The Ego calls this "Evil" because it feels like **The Rot**.

  - *Perception:* Boredom, Stagnation, Weakness, "The Nanny State."

### Bottom Right Quadrant (The Protection Trap)

- **Coordinate:** (-0.5 υ, -0.5 ψ)

- **Objective Name:** The Greater Evil (Selfish Indifference).

- **Subjective Label:** **P-LG (Subjective Lesser Good)**.

- **The Inversion:** The Ego calls this "Good" because it feels like a **Shield**.

  - *Perception:* Necessary Evil, "Law and Order," "Protecting our Own."

## 3. The Zone 1 Boundary: The Human Horizon

**Definition:** This is the realm of **Objective Human Experience**. At this distance (1.0), we measure the actual outcome of the vector, stripped of the Ego's perceptual distortion.

### Top Left Quadrant (True Virtue)

- **Coordinate:** (+1.0 υ, +1.0 ψ)

- **Name:** **The Greater Good**.

- **State:** Flow / Joy.

- **Definition:** High Proactive Will applied to Universal Benefit. Creating value for everyone.

### Top Right Quadrant (True Vice)

- **Coordinate:** (-1.0 υ, +1.0 ψ)

- **Name:** **The Greatest Lie**.

- **State:** Greed / Ambition (High Tension).

- **Definition:** High Proactive Will applied to Self-Benefit. Creating value only for the self.

### Bottom Left Quadrant (True Stability)

- **Coordinate:** (+1.0 υ, -1.0 ψ)

- **Name:** **The Lesser Good**.

- **State:** Peace / Contentment.

- **Definition:** Passive Will allowing Universal Benefit. Maintaining the safety of the whole.

### Bottom Right Quadrant (True Nihilism)

- **Coordinate:** (-1.0 υ, -1.0 ψ)

- **Name:** **The Greater Evil**.

- **State:** Depression / Void.

- **Definition:** Passive Will allowing Self-Benefit (or Self-Pity). The collapse of value.

- **(-3, +3):** Dogma.
