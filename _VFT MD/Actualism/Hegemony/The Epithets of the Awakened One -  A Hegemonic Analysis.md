Below are the Ten Principal Epithets (*dasa-bāla*) and other major titles, analyzed through the lens of the Framework.

## **The Ten Principal Epithets**

### **1. Tathāgata (The Thus-Gone / The Thus-Come)**

- **Traditional Meaning:** One who has gone (to Enlightenment) and returned (to teach) in the same way as the predecessors. It implies a connection to an absolute reality "as it is" (*Tathata*).

- **Hegemonic Vector Analysis:**

  - **Vector Coordinates:** \[+1, +1\] (The Ideal).

  - **Analysis:** This epithet describes the **Perfect Loop**. The entity has traversed the full *Psochic Hegemony*—departing the mundane (Unobservable), verifying the Truth (Observable), and returning. It signifies a being who exists in the state of "Is-ness," where there is no dissonance between the *Map* (Worldview) and the *Territory* (Reality).

### **2. Arahant (The Worthy One / The Foe-Destroyer)**

- **Traditional Meaning:** One who has destroyed the "foes" (mental defilements/passions) and is worthy of offerings. The fully liberated being.

- **Hegemonic Vector Analysis:**

  - **Conflict Resolution:** This represents the successful execution of **Conflict Resolution** against the *Minimiser* forces of the self (Greed, Anger, Delusion).

  - **State:** The Arahant has zeroed out the "Hum" (Dissonance). The *Resonance* is pure. The "Foe" destroyed is the *Lie* (Answer \< 1).

### **3. Samyak-saṃbuddha (The Perfectly Self-Enlightened One)**

- **Traditional Meaning:** One who has discovered the Truth by themselves (without a teacher in that life) and teaches it to others.

- **Hegemonic Vector Analysis:**

  - **Mechanism:** Infinite Intelligence.

  - **Analysis:** This denotes the capacity to derive the *Kanon* (the Standard of Truth) directly from the observation of Reality, without an intermediary. It is the ultimate manifestation of the *Maximiser* drive—seeking total integration of all knowledge (Observable + Unobservable) into a unified theory.

### **4. Vijjā-caraṇa-sampanno (Endowed with Knowledge and Conduct)**

- **Traditional Meaning:** Perfect in theory (understanding) and practice (morality/behavior).

- **Hegemonic Vector Analysis:**

  - **Equation:** \$Knowledge (\\upsilon) \\times Conduct (\\psi) = Resonance\$.

  - **Analysis:** This epithet confirms that "Truth" is not just intellectual; it is kinetic. High *Knowledge* without *Conduct* leads to the "Hypocrite's Vector" (The Lie). This title represents the perfect synchronization of **Phronesis** (Practical Wisdom) and **Action**.

### **5. Sugata (The Well-Gone / The Happy One)**

- **Traditional Meaning:** One who has gone to a good state (Nirvana) and speaks only what is right and beneficial.

- **Hegemonic Vector Analysis:**

  - **Trajectory:** Positive Slope.

  - **Analysis:** A trajectory analysis of the Sugata vector shows it moves exclusively away from the *Nihilistic Singularity* (Suffering/Entropy) and toward the *Attractor of Order* (Bliss). It implies that the "Good" is not subjective, but a measurable state of low-entropy being.

### **6. Lokavidū (Knower of Worlds)**

- **Traditional Meaning:** One who understands the nature of the universe (cosmology) and the nature of sentient beings (psychology).

- **Hegemonic Vector Analysis:**

  - **Scope:** Infinite.

  - **Analysis:** Master of the *Vector Field*. The Lokavidū perceives the invisible field lines (cause and effect, karma, social forces) that govern the "Worlds." This is the analyst who sees the *Psochic Hegemony* in its entirety—the "Code of Reality."

### **7. Anuttaro Purisa-damma-sārathi (Unsurpassed Trainer of Tameable Men)**

- **Traditional Meaning:** The incomparable charioteer (guide) for those who are ready to be trained.

- **Hegemonic Vector Analysis:**

  - **Function:** Trajectory Warfare / Correction.

  - **Analysis:** The Buddha acts as the external *Kanon*. When a subjective vector is misaligned (e.g., in the "Greed Zone"), the Sārathi applies the precise "Correction Vector" to realign the individual toward the Greater Good. "Tameable" implies the subject must possess a non-zero *Willingness* coefficient.

### **8. Satthā Deva-manussānaṃ (Teacher of Gods and Humans)**

- **Traditional Meaning:** The universal teacher, instructing beings in higher and lower realms.

- **Hegemonic Vector Analysis:**

  - **Universal Applicability.**

  - **Analysis:** The Truth (Answer = 1) is scale-invariant. It applies to "Gods" (High-Energy/Unobservable entities) and "Humans" (Observable/Material entities) equally. The laws of the Hegemony are constant across all frames of reference.

### **9. Buddha (The Awakened One)**

- **Traditional Meaning:** One who has woken up from the dream of ignorance to see Reality.

- **Hegemonic Vector Analysis:**

  - **State:** The Observer.

  - **Analysis:** The fundamental transition from *Delusionism* (The Lie) to *Realism* (The Truth). To be "Awake" is to perceive the *Vector Field* directly, rather than the "Cover" or "Narrative" draped over it.

### **10. Bhagavā (The Blessed One / The Lord)**

- **Traditional Meaning:** One endowed with fortune, glory, and spiritual power.

- **Hegemonic Vector Analysis:**

  - **Resource State:** Maximum Potential Energy.

  - **Analysis:** The accumulation of "Merit" acts as stored potential energy in the system, allowing the Bhagavā to exert influence on the field without resistance.
