**The 10 Synthesized Axioms of Hegemonic Navigation**

1.  The Axiom of Aletheia (Unconcealment):

2.  The Axiom of the Greater Good (The Prime Vector):
    The optimal path is the "Greater Good" (+υ, +ψ): a proactive will ("Do") applied for the benefit of all ("Everyone"). (Kanon: AFfJoI, Seal of Solomon)

3.  The Axiom of the Greater Evil (The Nihilistic Singularity):
    The most destructive vector is the "Greater Evil" (-υ, -ψ): a suppressive will ("Do Not") applied for the benefit of the self ("Only Me"). This is the vector of nihilism, apathy, and the claim that "no answer exists." (Kanon: AFfJoI, Seal of Solomon)

4.  The Axiom of the Grifter (The Greatest Lie):
    Beware the "Lesser Evil" (+υ, -ψ): a suppressive will ("Do Not") disguised for the benefit of all ("Everyone"). This is the vector of the "Fake Maximiser" and the "Grifter's Opportunity"—using the language of the Greater Good to enforce a destructive or selfish inaction. (Kanon: AFfJoI, Seal of Solomon; TMP 4.1)

5.  The Axiom of Inquiry (The Anti-Vector to Evil):
    Protect your worldview integrity by subjecting all new ideas to a rigorous, multi-perspective inquiry (Utilitarian, Strategic, Systemic, Adversarial) before acceptance. A polluted worldview is reduced in size and integrity. (Kanon: AFfJoI, Part 2, Part 5)

6.  The Axiom of Tension (The Internal Kanon):
    Use your internal state as a measuring instrument. A "good" idea (aligned with the Greater Good) generates a harmonious, low-resistance feeling. A "bad" idea (aligned with the Greater Evil) generates a distinct, high-strain tension. Do not passively accept this tension; use it as a signal to begin analysis. (Kanon: AFfJoI, Part 1)

7.  The Axiom of Harmonia (The Resolution Vector):
    The goal of judgment is not condemnation, but resolution. Seek the "Common Ground" within an opposing idea and work to synthesize a new vector that moves both worldviews toward the "Greater Good" (+υ, +ψ). (Kanon: AFfJoI, Part 6)

8.  The Axiom of Delusionism (The Minimiser Tactic):
    Recognize that the primary weapon of a Minimiser is not the bomb, but the narrative. They seek to manufacture "strategic exhaustion" and "epistemic nihilism" by making the very concept of "facts" irrelevant. (Kanon: TMP 1.2)

9.  The Axiom of the Hum (The Detection Method):
    Listen for the "hum": the disproportionate, illogical, and chaotic reaction to a "Greater Good" policy. This reaction is the tactical signature of a Minimiser vector at work. (Kanon: TMP 1.4)

10. The Axiom of Phronesis (The Wielder's Will):
    This map is a compass, not a destination. The "Infinite Intelligence" (the wielder) retains the sole power and responsibility of practical wisdom to choose the path. (Kanon: Alethekanon Protocol)
