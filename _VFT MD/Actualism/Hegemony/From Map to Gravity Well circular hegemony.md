### Part 2: The Mathematical Formulas

Here are the step-by-step formulas to perform the conversion for any point (υ, ψ).

#### Step 1: Calculate the Radius (r)

The radius r in this new system is a direct, geometric measure of a sýstēma's total strain (). It represents the "moral and energetic distance" from the ideal state of the Greater Good.

Formula:

r = sqrt((1 - υ)\^2 + (1 - ψ)\^2)

- **At the Greater Good (1,1):** r = sqrt((1 - 1)\^2 + (1 - 1)\^2) = 0. The strain is zero. This is the center of the well.

- **At the Greater Evil (0,0):** r = sqrt((1 - 0)\^2 + (1 - 0)\^2) = sqrt(2) ≈ 1.414. This is the maximum possible distance, representing maximum strain. This is the outer edge of the graph.

- **At the Neutral Origin (0.5, 0.5):** r = sqrt((1 - 0.5)\^2 + (1 - 0.5)\^2) = sqrt(0.5) ≈ 0.707.

#### Step 2: Calculate the Angle (θ)

The angle θ represents the "character" or "archetype" of the point's deviation from the ideal. It tells you the *nature* of the strain.

Formula:

θ = atan2(ψ - 1, υ - 1)

The atan2(y, x) function correctly calculates the angle in radians for any quadrant. The result can then be converted to degrees.

- **At the Lesser Good (0, 1):** θ = atan2(1 - 1, 0 - 1) = atan2(0, -1) = 180°. This angle points directly "left" from the center, along the axis of pure Inaction (-ψ direction).

- **At the Lesser Evil (1, 0):** θ = atan2(0 - 1, 1 - 1) = atan2(-1, 0) = -90° or 270°. This angle points directly "down" from the center, along the axis of pure Selfishness (-υ direction).

- **At the Greater Evil (0, 0):** θ = atan2(0 - 1, 0 - 1) = atan2(-1, -1) = -135° or 225°. This angle points diagonally "down and left," representing the combination of Inaction and Selfishness.
