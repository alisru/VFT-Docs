# Guide to Drawing the Psochic Hegemony Graph

Follow this protocol to render the Map of Ideas (υ, ψ) with geometric fidelity.

All points are to be rendered as dots on the graph on their respective coordinates

All font is to be size 10, white on dark or dark on white

### 1. Canvas & Coordinate Logic

- **Aspect Ratio:** 1:1 (Square).

- **Grid Bounds (Extents):** Draw axes extending from **+2.5** to **-2.5**. This provides whitespace for the labels outside the Zone 2 boundary.

- **The Inversion Rule (CRITICAL):**

  - **Horizontal Axis (υ - Morality):**

    - **LEFT** is Positive (+2.0): Universal Benefit ("Everyone").

    - **RIGHT** is Negative (-2.0): Self Benefit ("Me").

  - **Vertical Axis (ψ - Will):**

    - **TOP** is Positive (+2.0): Proactive Will (Create).

    - **BOTTOM** is Negative (-2.0): Suppressive Will (Destroy).

### 2. The Horizons (The Zones)

Draw two concentric squares centered on the origin (0,0).

- **Zone 1 (The Inner Horizon):** Draw a square at bounds **\[± 1.0\]**.

  - *Represents:* Individual/Subjective Reality.

- **Zone 2 (The Outer Horizon):** Draw a square at bounds **\[± 2.0\]**.

  - *Represents:* Societal/Strategic Reality.

### 3. The Objective Boundary (Zone 1 Corners)

Label the four corners of Zone 1 (the inner square at ± 1.0). These represent the "True" states stripped of ego.

- **Label Placement:** Text should be placed **just outside** and below the corner points.

- Point placement: Label;

  - **Top Left (+1.0, +1.0):** **The Greater Good** (Flow).

  - **Top Right (-1.0, +1.0):** **The Greatest Lie** (Greed).

  - **Bottom Left (+1.0, -1.0):** **The Lesser Good** (Peace).

  - **Bottom Right (-1.0, -1.0):** **The Greater Evil** (Void).

### 4. The Inner Traps (The 0.5 Ring)

Mark four points halfway to the inner boundary (0.5). These represent the Perceptual Inversions (Ego justifications).

- **Label Placement:** Offset text vertically to avoid clutter.

- Point placement;

  - **Inner TL (+0.5, +0.5): P-LE**

  - **Inner TR (-0.5, +0.5): P-GG**

  - **Inner BL (+0.5, -0.5): P-GE**

  - **Inner BR (-0.5, -0.5): P-LE**

### 5. The Strategic Extremes (Zone 2 Corners)

Label the four corners of Zone 2 (the outer square at ± 2.0). These represent the Systemic Manifestations.

- **Label Placement:** Place text **inside** the Zone 2 boundary (before the 2.0 to 2.5 margin). Aligned against the left or right respectively

- Point placement;

  - **Outer TL (+2.0, +2.0):** **JUSTICE**

  - **Outer TR (-2.0, +2.0):** **TYRANNY**

  - **Outer BL (+2.0, -2.0):** **STAGNATION**

  - **Outer BR (-2.0, -2.0):** **CHAOS**

### 6. The Emotional Coordinates (The Vectors)

Plot these key reference points inside the quadrants (The 0.8 Box).

- **Label Placement:** Place text **immediately on** the points.
  Point placement;

  - **Joy (+0.8, +0.8):** Top-Left Quadrant.

  - **Anger (-0.8, +0.8):** Top-Right Quadrant.

  - **Peace (+0.8, -0.8):** Bottom-Left Quadrant.

  - **Depression (-0.8, -0.8):** Bottom-Right Quadrant.

### 7. Zone 3 (The Triadic Matrix) *Optional*

For high-resolution mapping, divide the entire grid \[± 2.0\] into:

- **4 Horizontal Rows** (Heights of 1.0 unit).

- **8 Vertical Columns** (Widths of 0.5 units).
