# Psochic Hegemony & Basileia Survey Items

This survey is structured to guide participants from the "Personal/Innocent" sphere into the "Societal/Complex" sphere.

**The Axes for Reference:**

- **Vertical Axis:** Will / Energy (+Will = Authoritarian/Active; -Will = Passive/Libertarian)

- **Horizontal Axis:** Morality / Benefit (Left = Benefit Everyone/Truth; Right = Benefit Self/Deception)
