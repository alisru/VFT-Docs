## 1. THE CONSCIOUS PLANE (WHO)

The Observer / The Operator

Function: Identity, Agency, Intent. The focal point (\$C\$) that collapses the wave function.

Primary Input: The state of the Self relative to the System.

The senses of the Conscious Plane are **Meta-Senses**. They do not detect the outside world; they detect the *internal* status of the "I" and its relationship to the other planes.

1.  **Identity (Self-Recognition):** The fundamental sense of "I Am." The ability to distinguish the internal monologue from external noise.

2.  **Intent (Vector Direction /** \$\\psi\$**):** The proprioception of the Will. Sensing the "push" or "pull" of one's own desire. "Am I driving, or am I being driven?"

3.  **Agency (Capacity):** The sense of *Power*. The internal fuel gauge measuring available energy to affect change.

4.  **Attention (The Gaze):** The tactile sense of the mind "touching" a concept. The ability to feel where focus is directed.

5.  **Connection (The Network):** The sense of *The Other*. Detecting the presence of another Consciousness (Subject) versus a mere Object. (The "Someone is watching me" feeling).

6.  **Alignment (Moral Proprioception):** The gyroscopic sense of balance on the Hegemony. Sensing if one is "leaning" towards the Greater Good (+\$\\upsilon\$) or the Self (-\$\\upsilon\$).

7.  **Coherence (The State of Truth):** The overarching sense of integration. The feeling of "wholeness" when all other planes align, versus the fragmentation of "hypocrisy."

## 2. THE PHYSICAL PLANE (WHERE)

The Anchor / The Hardware

Function: Locality, Coordinate, Tangible Existence. The starting point of navigation.

Primary Input: Matter, Energy, and Space.

We expand beyond the basic 5 senses to include the **Internal Physical Senses** (Interoception) required for a biological machine to navigate gravity.

1.  **Sight (Photonic Input):** Detection of the electromagnetic spectrum (Light/Time).

2.  **Sound (Volumetric Input):** Detection of vibration in the medium (Atmosphere/Gravity-analogue).

3.  **Touch (Haptic Input):** Detection of surface boundary and texture (Matter).

4.  **Taste/Smell (Chemical Input):** Detection of molecular composition (Quality).

5.  **Vestibular (Balance/Gravity):** The sense of *Up and Down*. Orientation within the gravitational field.

6.  **Proprioception (Kinesthetic):** The sense of body position. Knowing where your limbs are in 3D space without looking.

7.  **Nociception (Damage/Strain):** The specific detection of tissue damage (Physical Pain) or thermal limits (Heat/Cold).

## 3. THE LYRICAL PLANE (WHY)

The Purpose / The Story

Function: Meaning, Resonance, Narrative Reason.

Primary Input: The "Vibe" or Narrative Arc.

This plane senses the "Music" of reality. It detects if the data "rhymes."

1.  **Meaning (Significance):** The sense that "this matters." The weight or gravity of a symbol.

2.  **Resonance (The Hum):** Sensing when an external idea vibrates at the same frequency as an internal truth.

3.  **Irony (Dissonance):** The sharp sense of a gap between expectation (intent) and reality (outcome).

4.  **Archetype (Recognition):** Instantly recognizing the "role" of a person or event (e.g., "He is the Villain," "This is a Trap").

5.  **Tone (Atmosphere):** Sensing the emotional "temperature" or "color" of a room or conversation.

6.  **Flow (Narrative Continuity):** The sense of pacing. Is the story moving too fast, dragging, or perfectly timed?

7.  **Resolution (Closure):** The deep satisfaction of a cycle ending or a chord resolving.

## 4. THE HISTORICAL PLANE (CAUSE)

Function: The Vector of the Past, Causality, Lineage.

2.  **Inertia (Momentum):** Sensing the "heaviness" of a tradition or a habit. How hard is it to change this trajectory?

3.  **Debt (Karmic Load):** The sense of owing or being owed. The imbalance in the ledger of exchange.

4.  **Lineage (Origin):** Sensing the source. "Where did this really come from?"

5.  **Hindsight (Pattern Recognition):** The recognition of a repeating loop. "I have been here before."

6.  **Regret/Nostalgia (Temporal Emotion):** The specific emotional textures of looking backward.

7.  **Legacy (Projection):** Sensing what will remain after the current moment passes.
