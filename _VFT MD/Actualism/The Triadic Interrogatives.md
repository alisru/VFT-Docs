\[THE LOGIC OF INQUIRY\]

To judge any concept, locate its "Node" and ask the specific Past-Tense Interrogative.

- **The Virtue (Optimism)** answers the Affirmative (The Presence).

- **The Vice (Pessimism)** is revealed by the Negative (The Absence/Opposite).

### HOW TO USE THIS MAP (THE INTERROGATION)

1\. Locate the Failure:

If a system is failing, identify where the friction is.

- Is it a failure of Definition? (Row 1: What).

- Is it a failure of Execution? (Row 2: Where).

- Is it a failure of Leadership? (Row 3: Who).

- Is it a failure of Purpose? (Row 4: Why).

**2. Identify the Gap (The Modal):**

- **The Fact Gap (Did):** They don't know the basic truth. (Plane 0).

- **The Vision Gap (Could):** They lack imagination/faith. (Plane 1).

- **The Moral Gap (Should):** They know but refuse to act correctly. (Plane 2).

- **The Hope Gap (Would):** They fear the outcome. (Plane 3).

3\. Run the "Opposite" Check:

To catch a lie, ask the Opposite Question.

- *Example (Justice):* Instead of asking "Who should lead?" (which invites rhetoric), ask **"Who should NOT lead?"** (which reveals bias and corruption).
