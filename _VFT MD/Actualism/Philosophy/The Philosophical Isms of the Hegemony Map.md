# The Philosophical Isms of the Hegemony Map

This document maps the philosophical "Isms" (Optimisms and Pessimisms) to the 16-point Hegemony Map, based on the specific sub-node structure defined by the Architect.

## 1. The Structure

The map is divided into four main quadrants (Large Circles), each containing four sub-nodes (Small Dots).

- **GG (Greater Good):** The Reality Cluster (Top Left)

- **LG (Lesser Good):** The Human/Spirit Cluster (Bottom Left)

- **LE (Lesser Evil):** The Social/Abstract Cluster (Top Right)

- **GE (Greater Evil):** The Base/Material Cluster (Bottom Right)

## 3. The Main Node Definitions (The "Itself" States)

These define the core characteristics of the four main quadrants themselves, separate from their sub-nodes.

- **Greater Good (GG) Itself:**

  - **Components:** History, Language, Empathy, Justice.

  - **Core Philosophy:** The pursuit of objective truth, shared meaning, and fair social structure.

- **Lesser Good (LG) Itself:**

  - **Components:** Temperance, Imagination, Faith, Charity.

  - **Core Philosophy:** The cultivation of the internal self, spiritual connection, and benevolent action.

- **Lesser Evil (LE) Itself:**

  - **Components:** Nihilism, Hatred, Dogma, Indulgence.

  - **Core Philosophy:** The rejection of meaning, active malice, closed-mindedness, and lack of self-control.

- **Greater Evil (GE) Itself:**

  - **Components:** Apathy, Corruption, Deceit, Erasure.

  - **Core Philosophy:** The passive allowance of harm, the perversion of systems, the distortion of communication, and the destruction of truth.
