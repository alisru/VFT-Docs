## Part 2: The Physics of Interaction (Possigravity)

How do these planes interact? They are not static; they are dynamic fields governed by the force of **Possigravity**, generated by the Driver (Q1).

### 1. The Possibility Space as a Surface
Each individual (Q1) is attached to an **uncountably infinite surface** of their own possibility space. This is the raw material of the "What if?" (Q2).

**"Certainty" = Mass.**
When the Driver establishes a clear Intent ("I will do X"), it assigns **Mass** to a specific coordinate in the Possibility Plane. This creates a **Gravity Well** that physically bends **ALL 7 Planes** of reality toward the center:

1.  **Bending the Driver (Who - Self-Reinforcement):** The gravity acts on the identity itself. The "Who" becomes denser, stronger, and more defined ("I am the one who does this"). This is the **Self-Reinforcement Loop**.
2.  **Bending the Possible (What):** The gravity collapses the infinite variance. The probability of the chosen outcome approaches 1.0, while all conflicting "What ifs" are pushed to the periphery.
3.  **Bending the Physical (Where):** Resources (Matter) are attracted to the certainty. The density of the intent pulls necessary physical components and locations into the locus.
4.  **Bending the Lyrical (Why):** The narrative bends. The "story" of the world realigns to justify the outcome, making it feel meaningful and inevitable.
5.  **Bending the Logical (How):** The method reveals itself. Impossible paths become visible "downhill" slopes. The path of least resistance realigns to point to the solution.
6.  **Bending the Historical (Cause):** The past is recontextualized. Previous random events are now seen as "necessary causes" leading to this moment.
7.  **Bending the Emotive (Effect):** The "Want" aligns with the "Need." The emotional weight of the outcome pulls the Driver forward with passion, reducing hesitation (Resistance).

### 3. Gravitational Feedback (Self-Reinforcement)
Once a gravity well is established, it generates a **positive feedback loop**.
*   The "well" lowers the energetic cost of action (Negentropy).
*   This efficiency attracts more **Positivity** (other aligned possibilities/resources).
*   The system begins to "feed itself," growing the mass of the Certainty without additional strain from the Driver. This is the state of **Flow**.
