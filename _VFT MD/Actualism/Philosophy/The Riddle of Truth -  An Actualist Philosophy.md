## **Emotional-Physics: The Mechanics of Ideals**

The only thing impossible to know is the next moment. For even you don't know what the you in a moment from now will think, but you can always investigate to determine what you were thinking in the past to a certain degree of working perfection, and the only thing you are able to do is to work towards an ideal("goal") to ensure you in a moment will do and think about the things you want to do and think about.

- If you have an ideal with no perceptible or unknown way of getting there, that elicits fear and anxiety.

- If you have no ideal then its boredom, if an ideal was taken away being possible its sadness and depression, if its path to the ideal is blocked thats anger and frustration.

- If your ideal is helped along thats joy and relief, if it's suddenly opened up thats excitement and when your ideal is met that is peace and happiness.

What you know at any point in time about something shapes your emotional responses to things.
