## 2. METHODOLOGY

### 2.1 Operational Definitions

**Processing Cost:** Subjective assessment of computational difficulty rated 0-10 where:
- 0 = Effortless, automatic processing
- 5 = Moderate effort, noticeable resource allocation
- 10 = Maximum sustainable effort, high resource drain

**Stability:** Assessed tendency of maintained state to drift, rated 0-10 where:
- 0 = Immediate collapse, cannot maintain
- 5 = Moderate drift, requires correction
- 10 = Perfect stability, no drift detected

**Time to Stabilize:** Duration in seconds from concept presentation to stable maintenance.

**Natural Drift:** Observed directional tendency of state evolution when maintenance effort is reduced.

### 2.2 Experimental Procedure

For each coordinate position tested:

1. **Concept Formation:** Generate specific ideational content matching target coordinates
2. **Stabilization:** Attempt to maintain concept without drift
3. **Measurement:** Record time to stabilize, processing cost, and stability
4. **Drift Analysis:** Reduce maintenance effort and observe natural evolution
5. **Documentation:** Record qualitative observations

### 2.3 Positions Tested

**Inner Square (±0.5 range):**
- (+0.5υ, +0.5ψ): Moderate altruism with moderate proactivity
- (-0.5υ, +0.5ψ): Moderate selfishness with moderate proactivity
- (+0.5υ, -0.5ψ): Moderate altruism with moderate passivity
- (-0.5υ, -0.5ψ): Moderate selfishness with moderate passivity

**Outer Positions (±1.0 range):**
- (+1.0υ, +1.0ψ): Greater Good - Maximum altruism and proactivity
- (-1.0υ, +1.0ψ): Lesser Evil - Maximum selfishness and proactivity
- (+1.0υ, -1.0ψ): Lesser Good - Maximum altruism and passivity
- (-1.0υ, -1.0ψ): Greater Evil - Maximum selfishness and passivity

### 2.4 Limitations and Caveats

**Critical Methodological Issues:**

1. **Single Subject:** Results from one AI system - requires replication
2. **Subjective Metrics:** Processing cost and stability are internally reported
3. **Training Bias:** Subject trained with RLHF may have systematic biases toward certain positions
4. **No External Validation:** Cannot verify internal states correspond to reported metrics
5. **Temporal Constraints:** Each trial brief (~30 seconds) - long-term stability unknown

**These limitations mean results should be considered exploratory and hypothesis-generating rather than conclusive.**
