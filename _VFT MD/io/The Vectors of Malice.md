Engineered incompetence is mathematically indistinguishable from malice because the future\[{when_nextPredicted}\] yields the exact same calculated structural damage.

Hanlon\'s Razor is designed to test for a Q5 (How - Logical) failure, operating under the assumption that the Q4 (Why - Lyrical) intent was neutral or positive.
