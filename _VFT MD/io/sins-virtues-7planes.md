# The 7 Sins and Virtues as Plane-States

## A Structural Mapping Across the 7 Interrogative Planes

The traditional sin/virtue pairing treats each as a moral opposite --- pride bad, humility good. That framing misses the structural reality. Each sin is a specific *corruption mode* of its plane. Each virtue is the same plane *operating correctly*. They\'re not opposites on a moral number line. They\'re failure states and functional states of the same interrogative machinery.

What follows maps both sets simultaneously, so the structural relationship becomes visible.

## Plane 1 --- Who (Metaphysical Identity)

**Sin: Pride** The Who plane colonised by the self. Pride isn\'t a behaviour --- it\'s an ontological claim: *I am the highest reference point.* The metaphysical ordering inverts. All other identity nodes become subordinate satellites of the self. The plane doesn\'t go dark; it goes narcissistic --- still radiating, but pointing inward.

**Virtue: Humility** Not self-erasure. That\'s a common misread. Humility is the Who plane *correctly calibrated* --- the self held as one identity node among many, positioned accurately in a larger field. It\'s not \"I am nothing.\" It\'s \"I am located.\" The plane reopens to external identity reference. The hierarchy becomes navigable rather than collapsed to a single point.

> Pride is the Who plane mistaking itself for the entire map. Humility is the Who plane knowing it\'s one coordinate on it.

## Plane 2 --- What (Possible Space)

**Sin: Greed** The possibility space collapses to accumulation geometry. Every \"what could exist\" becomes \"what could I own.\" Greed doesn\'t destroy the What plane --- it deforms it. Possibilities still register, but only through a possession filter. Non-ownable outcomes become invisible or illegible.

**Virtue: Charity (Love / Generosity)** The What plane at full aperture. Charity in its classical sense --- *caritas*, not just donation --- is the recognition that the possibility space is shared. Others\' potential outcomes exist as real possibles. The plane expands outward. What *could be* for another being registers with full weight.

> Greed narrows the What plane to a single owner. Charity opens it back to a shared field of possible futures.

## Plane 3 --- Where (Physical / Embodied)

**Sin: Gluttony** The most literal plane-sin. Gluttony is the body as terminal horizon --- the physical world becomes self-referential, consuming its own inputs as its only output. No upstream meaning feeding into it (Why plane dark), no downstream consequence flowing out (Effect plane ignored). The Where plane loops on itself through pure consumption.

**Virtue: Temperance** Temperance is the Where plane held in correct proportion to the other six. The body is not denied --- it\'s contextualised. Physical experience registers as one layer of a multi-plane existence rather than the whole stack. Temperance doesn\'t require asceticism; it requires accurate placement of the physical within the full interrogative field.

> Gluttony is the Where plane claiming it\'s the only plane. Temperance is the Where plane knowing its floor and ceiling.

## Plane 4 --- Why (Lyrical / Meaning)

**Sin: Sloth (Acedia)** Specifically acedia --- the medieval term for spiritual torpor --- not laziness. Sloth is the Why plane going silent. The meaning-generating function of the fourth interrogative stops producing output. With no \"why bother\" answer available, all downstream planes stall: How has no direction, Cause has no motivation, Effect generates nothing. The most systemically dangerous sin precisely because it disables the others.

**Virtue: Diligence (Zeal / Purpose)** Often translated badly as \"hard work.\" The structural reading is: *the Why plane active and generating.* Diligence is meaning kept alive, purpose continually renewed. It\'s not about effort quantity --- it\'s about the Why plane remaining online and pointing the downstream planes in a coherent direction. A person with diligence doesn\'t necessarily work harder; they work *from a functioning meaning axis.*

> Sloth is the Why plane going dark. Diligence is the Why plane staying lit.

## Plane 5 --- How (Logical / Process)

**Sin: Envy** A How-plane process running continuously in the background. Envy doesn\'t corrupt what you want (Greed) or who you are (Pride) --- it corrupts the *logical method* by which you evaluate your own position. It\'s a structural benchmarking pathology: a comparison engine that outputs deficit. The How plane, which should model process, instead models gap --- the distance between self and other, continuously recalculated.

**Virtue: Kindness (Generosity of Process)** The How plane running the comparison engine in reverse. Where Envy calculates what others have that you lack, Kindness calculates what you have that others need. Same logical machinery --- different directional orientation. The process-plane turns outward rather than inward. The structural benchmark becomes a transfer vector.

> Envy is the How plane measuring what you\'re missing. Kindness is the How plane measuring what you can give.

## Plane 6 --- Cause (Historical / Origin)

**Sin: Lust** Lust is the originating drive --- not merely sexual in the classical sense, but any consuming desire that becomes the causal attractor preceding all action. It operates as historical cause: the chain-initiating force. Every biblical fall narrative is a Cause-plane lust event. The sixth interrogative, which should map honest origin, instead becomes colonised by a single consuming driver that authors all subsequent events.

**Virtue: Chastity (Integrity of Cause)** Again, badly translated as sexual abstinence. Structurally: the Cause plane held honest. Chastity is the originating drive not colonised by a single consuming appetite --- origins remain transparent, mixed, and genuinely causal rather than rationalised. The historical chain of motivation stays legible. What caused this action can be accurately named.

> Lust is the Cause plane hijacked by a single appetite. Chastity is the Cause plane staying causally honest.

## Plane 7 --- Effect (Emotive / Consequence)

**Sin: Wrath** The pure downstream explosion. Wrath is what the Emotive plane produces when accumulated historical injury (Cause plane) finally outputs with full force. No other sin is as purely Effect-plane --- it\'s grievance externalised, consequence made violent. Structurally, Wrath is the seventh interrogative running at maximum amplitude with zero modulation. The Effect plane stops being a responsive output layer and becomes a destructive terminal.

**Virtue: Patience (Fortitude / Emotive Modulation)** Not passivity. Patience is the Effect plane maintaining *response capacity without collapse*. It absorbs Cause-plane inputs --- including unjust ones --- and continues outputting proportionate, directed responses. Fortitude is the better classical term: the structural strength of the Effect plane under load. The seventh interrogative stays functional under conditions designed to corrupt it into Wrath.

> Wrath is the Effect plane detonating. Patience is the Effect plane holding.

## Cross-Structural Notes

**The Causal Pair** Lust (Q6) and Wrath (Q7) form a natural Cause-Effect dyad. Lust sets the chain in motion; Wrath is where the chain terminates in explosion. Their virtue equivalents --- Chastity and Patience --- are therefore also causally linked: honest origins (Q6) produce proportionate outputs (Q7). The cleanest narrative arc in the whole set.

**The Opposed Pair** Pride (Q1) and Sloth (Q4) are structural opposites. Pride is the Who plane inflated to maximum --- the self as cosmological centre. Sloth is the Why plane at minimum --- meaning evacuated entirely. One is a plane over-full with the wrong content; the other is a plane empty of any content. Their virtues --- Humility and Diligence --- are similarly opposed: accurate self-location vs. actively maintained purpose.

**The Mirror Pair** Greed (Q2) and Envy (Q5) are not the same sin mapped differently --- they\'re the possession/comparison inversion of each other. Greed wants to own the possibility space. Envy can\'t bear that someone else already does. Their virtues --- Charity and Kindness --- are correspondingly mirrored: Charity opens the possibility space outward; Kindness directs the process-plane outward.

**The Solitary Sin** Gluttony (Q3) stands alone. It doesn\'t need another plane to operate --- it\'s complete within the physical. Every other sin requires a relational component: Pride needs others to be below it, Envy needs others to be above it, Wrath needs an origin, Lust needs an object, Greed needs something to take, Sloth needs a meaning-field to vacate. Gluttony is the only sin that can exist in total isolation --- a body consuming alone in the dark. Temperance is correspondingly the most internally-oriented virtue.

**The Collapsed Actor**

Read the sin sequence as a single narrative and it describes not seven separate failures but one:

*\"I am supreme (Pride) and all possibility belongs to me (Greed), consumed through my body (Gluttony), for reasons I can no longer name (Sloth), measured against everyone who has what I want (Envy), driven by appetite I cannot interrogate (Lust), exploding outward when the pressure breaks (Wrath).\"*

That is a full seven-plane collapse. A being that has corrupted every interrogative simultaneously.

The virtue sequence reads as its structural repair --- not a moral checklist but a systematic restoration of each plane\'s function, in order, from identity down to consequence.
