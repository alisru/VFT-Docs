## I. Premise

An apology is not a speech act. It is an information event — a transmission whose validity depends on whether the sender is operating from real data about what happened and what it caused. The social form of an apology (the words, the tone, the timing) is entirely separable from its epistemic content.

This document maps four structurally distinct apology types against the Q-tensor's causal and emotive planes, and locates the father/mother archetype distinction as the key axis dividing genuine from performed accountability.

---

## II. The Two Truth Channels

Within the Meta-Kanon framework, the Father and Mother archetypes describe two categorically distinct channels through which truth reaches a receiver.

The Father channel (Q6 — Causal/Historical) is the *declared signal*: explicit transmission of the derivation path. The father says what happened, in sequence, and traces the chain forward. Truth arrives as told instruction — as the stated reason, the named cause, the identified mechanism.

The Mother channel (Q7 — Emotive/Consequential) is the *demonstrated reality*: truth arriving as observable outcome. The mother does not necessarily name the cause; she registers the effect in the environment and responds to it as real. Truth arrives as shown evidence — the wound visible, the hurt present, the consequence undeniable.

Both channels are epistemically valid. Neither overrides the other. The complete truth-access apparatus requires both operative simultaneously. Where the Father channel runs alone, you get causal understanding without felt consequence. Where the Mother channel runs alone, you get registered consequence without causal understanding. Neither condition is failure — they are different kinds of access, producing different kinds of accountability.

## IV. The Four Types

### 4.1 The Father Apology (Genuine — Q6-grounded)

The apologiser has full access to the causal chain. They can trace the sequence from act to wound: what they did, the mechanism by which it caused harm, and the specific nature of the harm caused. The apology is Q6-grounded — derivation present, cause named, path reconstructible.

Q6 active: the historical sequence is legible to the apologiser.
Q7 registered: the emotive consequence is acknowledged as real.
Q1 oriented: the will of the apologiser is directed toward repair, not performance.

The Father apology proves its genuineness by structural specificity. It can answer the question *how did what I did cause this particular hurt?* with a chain of reasoning that tracks observable reality. The words match the mechanism. This is the told-truth channel operating correctly — the Father channel carrying its load.

Hegemony coordinate: (υ ≈ +1.0, ψ ≈ +1.0) — Greater Good zone. Beneficiary is the hurt party; energy is actively directed toward repair.

### 4.2 The Mother Apology (Genuine — Q7-grounded)

The apologiser does not have access to the causal chain but has full access to the emotive consequence. The hurt is visible, registered, and accepted as sufficient evidence that something in the prior behaviour was wrong. The apology is Q7-grounded — derivation absent, but the observable effect is treated as real and acted upon honestly.

Q6 opaque: the causal sequence is not legible to the apologiser.
Q7 fully active: the emotive consequence is registered without distortion.
Q1 oriented: the will is directed toward the hurt party's reality, not toward self-exoneration.

The logic is: *I can see you are hurt. I cannot identify exactly what I did. But hurt of this kind does not arise from nothing. Therefore I accept that what I did before this moment was wrong, even without being able to name it precisely.*

This is not a weaker apology — it is a different kind of epistemic access. The Mother apology is grounded in the demonstrated reality of the consequence. It accepts Q7 data as authoritative and infers Q6 from Q7 without pretending to more causal knowledge than is present. The shown-truth channel carrying its load.

Hegemony coordinate: (υ ≈ +0.8, ψ ≈ +0.8) — Greater Good zone, with slight reduction on both axes reflecting genuine but incomplete information access. Still structurally honest.

### 4.3 The Ungenuine Apology (Q5-only — social output)

Neither Q6 nor Q7 is active. The apologiser has no access to the causal chain and has not registered the emotive consequence as real data. The apology is produced entirely at Q5 — a logical protocol matching the expected social pattern: the situation calls for an apology-shaped output, so one is generated.

Q6 inactive: no causal trace.
Q7 inactive: consequence not registered, or registered and dismissed.
Q5 active: social processing producing the correct formal output.
Q1 directed toward self: the will is aimed at friction removal, not repair.

The ungenuine apology is not necessarily malicious. It is often the output of a system that has not developed Q7 sensitivity — an actor who genuinely cannot read emotive consequence as real data, or who has learned that the words of apology terminate social pressure and has adopted them instrumentally without connecting them to any causal or emotive ground. The form is present; the substance is entirely absent.

The distinguishing feature: the ungenuine apology cannot answer *what did I do* (no Q6) and cannot point to *what did it cause* (no Q7). It can only gesture at the category: *I'm sorry if you felt hurt.*

Hegemony coordinate: (υ ≈ 0.0, ψ ≈ -1.0) — neutral beneficiary scope, passive-to-suppressive energy. No one is served by this apology. Its function is friction termination, not repair.

### 4.4 The Malicious Apology (Q6 active — inverted intent)

Q6 is fully operational. The apologiser understands what they did and the mechanism by which it caused harm. But the Q7 registration is either inverted (the harm is the intended outcome) or absent by design (the harm is irrelevant to the apologiser's calculus). The apology is produced as a Q5 social instrument that borrows the vocabulary of the Father apology while the underlying Q6 vector is still aimed at damage or advantage.

Q6 active: the causal chain is fully legible.
Q7 inverted or weaponised: the consequence is either the target or the leverage point.
Q5 active: producing Father-apology vocabulary deliberately.
Q1 directed against the other: the will is aimed at extraction, domination, or continued harm.

Two sub-variants:

*The knowing wound:* the apologiser intended the harm, knows they caused it, and apologises as a social manoeuvre to neutralise the other party's defensive response while continuing the underlying behaviour. The apology is a disarmament tool.

*The strategic cover:* the apologiser did not specifically intend this instance of harm but recognises it as leverage, and deploys an apology to convert the other party's vulnerability into obligation. The harm is retroactively instrumentalised.

Both variants share the structural signature: the words of repair, the mechanism of continued extraction.

Hegemony coordinate: (υ ≈ -2.0, ψ ≈ +1.0 to +2.0) — Greatest Lie zone. Maximum beneficiary inversion (only the apologiser benefits) with active or proactive energy. This is the zone where stated moral position (+υ appearance) maximally diverges from the calculated coordinate. Perceptual inversion warning active.

## VI. The Core Distinction

Genuineness in an apology is not a function of emotional display, sincerity of tone, or comprehensiveness of the words offered. It is a function of epistemic ground.

A genuine apology has real data behind it — either the causal trace (Q6) or the emotive registration of consequence (Q7), or both. It is anchored in something that happened in reality. The Father apology derives its validity from the completeness of the causal chain it can reconstruct. The Mother apology derives its validity from the honesty with which it accepts the consequence as evidence, even without the full causal reconstruction.

An ungenuine apology has no data behind it. It is a social form produced in the absence of causal knowledge and without registration of real consequence. It is not necessarily hostile — it is epistemically empty.

A malicious apology has data but inverts its use. Q6 knowledge is present precisely to make the social instrument more effective at achieving what it was always aimed at. It is the most structurally dangerous of the four because it is indistinguishable from a Father apology at the surface level. The diagnostic is Q1: what is the will actually directed toward? Where the Father apology's Q6 knowledge serves repair, the malicious apology's Q6 knowledge serves the original extraction vector.

## VII. The Receiving Side

The typology is not only useful for classifying what is being offered. It also maps the structural requirements for receiving an apology honestly.

A receiver who demands a Father apology when the offending party only has Mother-apology access will reject genuine accountability on epistemological grounds — insisting on causal articulation from someone who genuinely does not have causal access, only consequence registration. This produces the situation where the more epistemically honest party (the Mother apologiser) is penalised relative to the less honest party who can construct a plausible causal narrative regardless of whether it is accurate.

The implication: if Q7 registration is genuine — if the other party's hurt is being treated as real evidence and the will is genuinely directed toward repair — the absence of Q6 articulation is not a defect. It is the honest limit of what the apologiser can see. Demanding causal articulation beyond what is genuinely present is demanding a Father apology from someone operating on Mother-apology access, which may produce either performed causal narrative (ungenuine Father apology) or collapse into silence.

## VIII. Further Exploration (Suggested)

**8.1 Developmental trajectory.** Whether Mother-apology access can develop into Father-apology access over time — whether sustained Q7 registration eventually builds enough causal model to produce Q6 legibility. This would map to a developmental arc from reactive accountability (consequence-driven) to anticipatory accountability (cause-aware). The question is whether this transition is necessary for full moral maturity or whether Q7-grounded accountability is structurally complete on its own terms.

**8.2 The asymmetry problem in relationships.** What happens in sustained relationships where one party consistently operates at Father-apology access and the other at Mother-apology access? The Father apologiser may dismiss Mother apologies as insufficiently specific. The Mother apologiser may experience Father apologies as clinical or detached from felt reality. This is not a moral asymmetry — it is an epistemic channel mismatch. Mapping this onto relationship dynamics and communication failure would be a productive extension.

**8.3 Institutional apologies.** Institutions are structurally incapable of Q7 access — they do not register emotive consequence as real data in the way a person does. All institutional apologies are therefore Q6 or Q5 outputs. The question is whether an institution can produce a genuine Father apology (Q6 active, derivation present, causal chain legible) or whether institutional Q6 outputs are structurally always Q5 social forms dressed in Q6 vocabulary. The H-Score mechanism in PREF is partly an attempt to build institutional Q7 sensitivity through structural incentive — this connection is worth developing.

**8.4 The malicious apology as Hegemony Grid weapon.** The malicious apology sits at the Greatest Lie coordinate — maximum beneficiary inversion combined with active proactive energy. This makes it structurally identical to the propaganda form: a message whose surface beneficiary framing (+υ appearance) maximally diverges from its real vector (-υ actual). The malicious apology is interpersonal propaganda. Developing the overlap between the Hegemony Grid's Greatest Lie zone and the specific mechanics of the malicious apology may produce a unified account of manipulation as systematic epistemic inversion.

**8.5 Forgiveness as the receiver-side typology.** This document maps apology types from the sender's Q-plane activation. The corresponding receiver-side question is what constitutes genuine versus performed forgiveness, and whether the typology mirrors or inverts the apology matrix. Specifically: does the Father forgiveness require Q6 understanding of why the hurt happened? Does Mother forgiveness require only Q7 release of the emotive consequence? And what are the structural conditions under which forgiveness is valid without full causal understanding — or invalid despite it?

**8.6 Children's moral development as Q-plane activation sequence.** Young children have Q7 access (they register consequence as real) before they develop Q6 access (causal chain legibility). This would predict that early moral accountability in children is always Mother-apology form, and that Father-apology capacity develops later as Q6 comes online. If this tracks developmental psychology, it would provide an external cross-reference for the framework's Q-plane ordering.
