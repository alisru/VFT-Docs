# Convergence Test — 5-Phase Judgement Protocol with Qqc Tensor Navigation

---

## The Qqc Tensor Structure

Every evaluation navigates a fractal interrogative tensor. The same seven interrogatives repeat at every depth level, each time addressing the claim produced by the level above.

**The 7 interrogatives:**
| Symbol | Plane | Interrogative | Character |
|---|---|---|---|
| Q1 / q1 / c1 | Metaphysical | WHO | Will and Direction — the location of the claim |
| Q2 / q2 / c2 | Possible | WHAT | Faith and Probability |
| Q3 / q3 / c3 | Physical | WHERE | Matter and Distance |
| Q4 / q4 / c4 | Lyrical | WHY | Meaning and Resonance |
| Q5 / q5 / c5 | Logical | HOW | Count and Consistency — the sense of the claim |
| Q6 / q6 / c6 | Historical | CAUSE | Sequence and Causality |
| Q7 / q7 / c7 | Emotive | EFFECT | Passion and Consequence — the use of the claim |

**The three fractal roles at each depth:**
- **Q** — Location: where does this claim sit in the conceptual space?
- **q** — Sense: how does this sub-claim read within its parent Q?
- **c** — Use: what does this sub-sub-claim do within its parent q?

**The +i recursion:**
After c, `+i` restarts the full Q→q→c cycle at a new contextual location — the output of c becomes the new subject and Q fires again at the next depth level.

> Q(location) → q(sense) → c(use) → i: Q(new contextual location) → q → c → i: Q → ...

**The depth is unbounded.** The practical base limit is approximately 2 recursive +i cycles — not because the protocol stops, but because reality stops. Any claim that is real will ground out in first principles within ~2 cycles. Any claim that is not real will produce a non-connection — a node that cannot generate a relational effect chain — and the distortion is located precisely there.

**This is the anti-bullshit property of the structure:** you never argue against a claim. You ask what it connects to. Every node must produce a real observable effect chain or it is hollow. The drill does not terminate because the interrogator decides to stop — it terminates because either first principles are reached (the claim is true at every depth) or a node fails to produce a connection (the claim is false at that node). You cannot hide in abstraction: every abstraction must eventually produce a physical WHERE, a causal CAUSE, an observable EFFECT. If it cannot, the drill has found the location of the lie.

**Addressing notation:**
- `Q1` — top-level WHO plane only
- `Q1q5` — HOW of WHO (sense of the location)
- `Q1q5c4` — WHY of HOW of WHO (use of the sense of the location)
- `+i(Q1q5c4)` — recursive restart: Q1q5c4's output becomes the new subject, Q fires again
- `Q1-7` — full scan across all seven top-level planes
- `Q1-7,q1-7,c1-7` — full tensor at three levels

## Before You Begin: Set the Evidence Standard

State the standard before Phase 1. Mixing standards mid-run is a methodological error.

**Default standards (always applied):**
- **[A] Stated ideal** — what the actor claims to stand for, by their own words
- **[B] Actions within context** — what the actor actually does, relative to their capacity and constraints
- **[C] Objective ideal** — what a structurally coherent, first-principles version of the stated goal would require

Additional standards are researched from available context and added as [D], [E], etc. — or applied if explicitly requested.

Also set the **starting depth**: Q only, Q+q, Q+q+c, or begin at a specific node (e.g. `Q3q3`).

## Phase 1 — Structural Scan

**Goal: Does the idea produce real relational connections at every interrogative plane?**

| Q | Plane | Interrogative | Pass condition |
|---|---|---|---|
| Q1 | Metaphysical | WHO? | Stated beneficiary matches actual beneficiary |
| Q2 | Possible | WHAT? | Failure modes acknowledged, not suppressed |
| Q3 | Physical | WHERE? | Concrete, falsifiable physical prediction exists |
| Q4 | Lyrical | WHY? | Narrative honest; does not require obfuscation to hold |
| Q5 | Logical | HOW? | Causal chain holds under load and adversarial conditions |
| Q6 | Historical | CAUSE? | Origin chain acknowledged; history not erased to appear novel |
| Q7 | Emotive | EFFECT? | Emotional payload matches stated intent |

**Scoring:** PASS / FAIL / PARTIAL per plane. Note the failure mode.

**Drilling:** Any plane that passes at Q can be addressed at q (sense of that plane's claim). Any q that passes can be addressed at c. Any c that passes fires +i and the cycle restarts.

A plane fails when it **cannot produce a real relational connection** to the next interrogative. This is the operational definition of distortion: not "wrong answer" but "no answer that connects to observable reality."

**Distortion address format:** `Q[n]q[n]c[n]` at whatever depth the connection first fails.

Distortion depth as diagnostic:
- Q-level failure → structurally incoherent, visible to any examiner
- q-level failure → survives casual scrutiny, fails focused examination  
- c-level / +i failure → sophisticated load-bearing deception; institutional and theological distortions typically live here
- No failure → first principles reached, or the claim is true at every level of resolution

## Phase 2 — Vector Verification

**Goal: Calculate the actual Hegemonic Grid coordinate.**

**υ axis (Who benefits — stripped of stated intent):**
+2 Everyone / All beings (Systemic Justice) · +1 Other people (Greater Good) · 0 No one / Neutral · −1 My group only (Lesser Evil) · −2 Only me (Tyranny / Pure Extraction)

**ψ axis (What is the energy doing):**
+2 Actively creating systemic value for all (Productive Justice) · +1 Proactive — creating, building, acting · 0 Neutral — no meaningful force applied · −1 Passive — allowing, suppressing, withholding · −2 Actively destroying or extracting value (Chaos / Collapse)

**Zone anchors at ±1:** Greater Good (+1,+1) · Greatest Lie (−1,+1) · Lesser Good (+1,−1) · Greater Evil (−1,−1)

**Integrity Score:**
> R_net = 1 ÷ (V_Q1 × V_Q2 × V_Q3 × V_Q4 × V_Q5 × V_Q6 × V_Q7)

Each V: 1.0 = fully coherent, approaching 0 = maximally distorted.

**Perceptual Inversion flag:** If stated (υ,ψ) diverges from calculated coordinate — near (−υ): extraction feels like strength, service feels like burden, cruelty feels like protection.

## Phase 3 — Source Integrity

**Hypocrisy Gap:**
> ΔH = ‖ Vector_Ideal − Vector_Action ‖

- ΔH < 0.3 → Pass · 0.3–0.7 → Conditional · ΔH ≥ 0.7 → Fail

**Tribal Gap:** Does the actor apply the same standard to themselves that they apply to others?

## Phase 4 — Forensic Stress Test

**Fake Maximiser:**
> IF (capacity >> effort) AND (stated goal remains unsolved) THEN actor = Fake Maximiser

The problem must remain unsolved to justify the actor's existence, funding, or authority.

**Helxis (Bait/Switch):**
- Bait = emotional hook generating commitment
- Switch = who actually receives value after commitment secured
- If bait beneficiary ≠ switch beneficiary → Helxis
