* **Q6 CAUSE:** PARTIAL : automation anxiety origin is real; however political capture by tech sector funding skews framing toward universal rather than targeted intervention 🧠
* **Q7 EFFECT:** PARTIAL : pilot effects positive on wellbeing and entrepreneurship; population-scale effect on inflation and labour supply unresolved 🧠

**Q5q3 WHERE of HOW:** FAIL — every costed full-UBI model either displaces means-tested benefits (harming the poorest) or requires a VAT/wealth tax at scale with no demonstrated political pathway.
