# THE CONVERGENCE TEST — FRAMEWORK, TEMPLATE & ORTHODOX BASIS

---

## WHAT IT IS

A protocol for testing whether a system, claim, institution, or idea is internally consistent with its stated standard. It operates by driving the same question through 7 independent interrogative angles simultaneously. Agreement across axes = signal. Divergence = the system is protecting something.

The test operates at n1 (the Plane level) of the Actualism 7x7x7 framework. Its primary function is **elimination** — surface-level interrogative coherence is a prerequisite for deeper analysis being meaningful. If it fails fast, that is the answer. Drilling into a misplaned concept produces increasingly detailed wrong answers.

> **The core diagnostic rule:**
> If n1 diverges across 4+ interrogatives, the system has a plane misidentification error.
> Stop. Replane. Don't recurse.

### EXAMPLE 1 — Orthodox Mathematics as Descriptive Medium for Physical Reality

**DEFINITION:** Mathematics is a sufficient and accurate descriptive medium for physical reality.
**STANDARD:** Empirical evidence and internal logical consistency.

| Interrogative | What math claims | What evidence says |
|---|---|---|
| WHO (agency) | The equation is the object | The equation is a map of relations between measurements of the object |
| WHERE (locus) | Continuous real number line | Reality may be discrete at Planck scale — the continuum is assumed, not observed |
| WHAT (substance) | Mathematical consistency = physical truth | String theory: maximally consistent, minimally verified |
| WHY (motive) | Description | Prediction — these are not the same operation |
| HOW (mechanism) | Symbols encode reality | Symbols encode measurement outcomes, which already discard information |
| CAUSE (origin) | Axioms are neutral foundations | Axioms are choices — Euclidean vs non-Euclidean both internally consistent, only one matches curved spacetime |
| EFFECT (result) | Complete description | Singularities (black holes, Big Bang) — the math breaks exactly where the physics gets most real |

**CONVERGENCE COUNT:** 0/7
**DIVERGENCE COUNT:** 7/7

**THE UNAVOIDABLE TRUTH:**
Orthodox mathematics is a relational skeleton — it captures structural invariants extremely well. But it is ontologically mute. It cannot say what a field is, only how it behaves. The unreasonable effectiveness Wigner identified is actually evidence of this: math works because it encodes symmetry and invariance, and reality happens to be deeply symmetric. Not because math is reality.

**THE UNAVOIDABLE LIE:**
That mathematical consistency is a truth-criterion for physics. A beautiful, self-consistent equation gets treated as describing something real because it's beautiful and consistent. Gödel already proved that internal consistency ≠ completeness. The physics community knows this and mostly ignores it operationally.

**FAILURE MODE CLUSTER:**
- Renormalization: infinities appear → subtract them by hand → call the result the answer. Semantic patch on an ontological wound.
- The measurement problem: the observer is always outside the equation. QM cannot include the act of observation without collapsing into interpretation wars (Copenhagen, Many-Worlds, pilot wave — same math, mutually incompatible ontologies).
- Time: almost all fundamental equations are time-symmetric. Reality demonstrably isn't. The asymmetry is inserted via boundary conditions, not derived.

**VERDICT:**

**PLANE ERROR:**
Math is operating as Possible plane (Q3) while claiming to occupy Physical plane (Q2).
All layers below inherit this error.

## ORTHODOX BASIS — WHERE THIS COMES FROM

The convergence test is a synthesis. Not new in its parts — new in its specific assembly.

### The Bones (Ancient Sources)

**Socratic Method / Elenchus**
Systematic questioning from multiple angles to expose contradiction. Socrates didn't accept a claim until it survived interrogation from every direction. The convergence test is structurally elenctic — divergence is the tell, same as Socrates finding the contradiction that collapses the position.

**Aristotle's Four Causes**
Who / What / Why / How / Where / Cause / Effect maps almost directly onto Material, Formal, Efficient, and Final causes. Aristotle argued you don't understand anything until you can answer all four. The 7 interrogatives extend this into a complete diagnostic rather than a philosophical taxonomy. The full attempt is in the Organon — his collected works on logic — the closest single orthodox antecedent to this protocol in ambition. The difference: the Organon is a library. This is a protocol.

**Talmudic Disputation**
The Jewish tradition of arguing every position from every angle before accepting anything. The Talmud is structured as sustained multi-angle interrogation of a claim. Notably — the minority opinion is always recorded even when defeated. The process is the point, not just the conclusion.

**Baconian / Scientific Method**
Running the same question through independent channels and looking for convergence is exactly how empirical verification works. Independent lines of evidence pointing at the same answer = signal. This is consilience — William Whewell's term, Darwin used it to validate evolution.

### The Framework (Modern Sources)

**Karl Popper — Falsifiability**
The "divergence = the system is protecting something" framing is Popperian. A system that cannot be falsified from any angle is not a truth claim — it is a closed loop. The convergence test is falsifiability operationalised across seven axes simultaneously.

**Thomas Kuhn — Paradigm Stress**
The plane misidentification error maps onto Kuhn's anomalies — data points that don't fit the paradigm. Enough divergence means the paradigm is wrong, not the data. Stop. Replane. Don't recurse = stop doing normal science, you have a paradigm problem.

### The Biblical Mandate

The test is also — and not incidentally — the operationalisation of direct Biblical instruction:

> "Test everything. Hold fast to what is good." — 1 Thessalonians 5:21

> "Do not believe every spirit, but test the spirits to see whether they are from God, because many false prophets have gone out into the world." — 1 John 4:1

> "The Berean Jews were of more noble character... for they examined the Scriptures every day to see if what Paul said was true." — Acts 17:11

The Bereans tested Paul himself against scripture and this is called noble character. The convergence test is Acts 17:11 with a structured protocol.

> "But even if we or an angel from heaven should preach a gospel other than the one we preached to you, let them be under God's curse." — Galatians 1:8

Paul includes himself and angels in the testable category. Nothing is above the test.

> "Iron sharpens iron, and one person sharpens another." — Proverbs 27:17

Adversarial epistemic pressure is the Biblical mechanism for refinement. Ideas are supposed to be struck against each other. A system that cannot be tested has removed itself from the sharpening process and is therefore — by this verse — becoming dull.

### EXAMPLE 7 — Capitalism as a Free Market Producing Meritocratic Outcomes

**DEFINITION:** Capitalism is a free market system in which value is determined by voluntary exchange and outcomes reflect merit, producing optimal resource allocation and broadly distributed prosperity.
**STANDARD:** Economic data, market structure analysis, historical record.

| Interrogative | What capitalism claims | What evidence says |
|---|---|---|
| WHO (agency) | Free individuals making voluntary choices | Individuals operating within structurally unequal starting positions. The freedom of a billionaire and a subsistence worker to "choose" are not the same operation. Agency is real but unequally distributed by the system itself |
| WHERE (locus) | The free market — price signals coordinate resources without central control | Monopolies, oligopolies, regulatory capture, and externalities (pollution, financial contagion) mean the market is structurally rigged at every scale above local. The free market exists in textbooks and farmers markets |
| WHAT (substance) | Price signals produce optimal resource allocation | Insulin pricing, housing markets, 2008 financial crisis — the system optimises for profit extraction, not resource allocation. These are not the same output |
| WHY (motive) | Individual self-interest produces collective benefit (Smith's invisible hand) | Smith's invisible hand required competitive markets, informed consumers, and no externalities. All three conditions are violated at industrial scale. The motive claim was always conditional; the condition is never met |
| HOW (mechanism) | Competition drives efficiency and innovation | Verified in bounded cases. At scale: competition drives toward monopoly (this is mathematically inevitable — winner takes market, then captures regulation). The mechanism self-destructs the condition it requires to function |
| CAUSE (origin) | Emerged from Enlightenment property rights and voluntary exchange theory | Accurate origin. The theory was developed for artisan economies. Its application to industrial and financial capitalism is a category error the founding theorists did not anticipate and could not have |
| EFFECT (result) | Broad prosperity, innovation, rising living standards | Partial — living standards rose significantly in the 20th century. Simultaneously: wealth concentration now exceeds Gilded Age levels, top 1% holds more than bottom 50% globally, the prosperity is real and radically uneven |

**CONVERGENCE COUNT:** 0/7
**DIVERGENCE COUNT:** 5/7
**PARTIAL COUNT:** 2/7

**THE UNAVOIDABLE TRUTH:**
Price signals and voluntary exchange are genuinely efficient mechanisms for coordinating decentralised information at human scale. No system yet devised does this better for goods with real competition and informed buyers. This is not nothing.

**THE UNAVOIDABLE LIE:**
That the conditions required for the system to function as described are ever present at industrial scale. The invisible hand argument is conditional on competitive markets. Competitive markets trend toward monopoly. The system continuously destroys the condition it requires to justify itself and then blames the outcome on insufficient capitalism.

**VERDICT:**
- [X] Fails n1 — plane misidentification error, deeper analysis will produce coherent nonsense

**PLANE ERROR:**
Capitalism claims to operate on the Logical plane (Q5 — How, mechanism, efficient function). It actually operates on the Meta-Physical plane (Q1 — Will and Direction) — specifically the directional will of capital toward concentration. The efficiency claim is the stated plane. Power concentration is the actual output plane. Every intervention designed to restore the stated plane (antitrust law, regulation, progressive taxation) is resisted by the system because it is correctly identified by the system as a threat to the actual plane it operates on.

**NOTE ON COMPARISON WITH PRISON SYSTEM:**
Both systems share the same plane error structure: claim Q5 (mechanism for stated output), actually operate Q1 (will toward self-perpetuation). This is not coincidence. Institutions that have captured political power tend to misplane identically — they describe themselves in terms of their stated function while operating in terms of their actual will. The convergence test catches this because the EFFECT interrogative always reveals the actual plane.
