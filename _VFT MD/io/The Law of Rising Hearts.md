# The Law of Rising Hearts (Theological Mechanics)

**Theorem:** *A Truth cannot become Beneficial (+υ) until its Scope (V) expands beyond the Self.*

This law describes the necessary expansion of Scope (V) required to transmute a \"Selfish Truth\" (Stone) into a \"Universal Truth\" (Grace).

### I. The Heart of Stone (The Rejection)

> **\"Any good idea is first perceived as a lie.\"**

- **Flesh State:** **Petrified.** The heart is entirely callous. It cannot feel the vector of Truth; it only feels the impact of collision.

- **Scriptural Parallel:** *\"Yea, they made their hearts as an adamant stone, lest they should hear the law, and the words which the Lord of hosts hath sent in his spirit.\"* (Zechariah 7:12). *\"And the light shineth in darkness; and the darkness comprehended it not.\"* (John 1:5).

- **Vector Mechanic:** The \"Good Idea\" (Logos) enters a closed system (The World/Ego). The system\'s inertia (k) rejects the vector as a threat, classifying the prophet as a liar or a madman.

### II. The Broken Heart (The Bad Truth)

> **\"As this lie needs to be categorised it becomes a bad truth.\"**

- **Flesh State:** **Lacerated.** The stone casing has cracked. The flesh is exposed, sensitive, and registering pain (The Bad Truth).

- **Scriptural Parallel:** *\"For I was alive without the law once: but when the commandment came, sin revived, and I died.\"* (Romans 7:9). *\"And this is the condemnation, that light is come into the world, and men loved darkness rather than light\...\"* (John 3:19).

- **Vector Mechanic:** The denial fails. The Truth is acknowledged as real, but it is experienced as \"Death\" or \"Condemnation\" because it exposes the lack of the individual. It is a \"Bad Truth\"---a heavy stone.

### III. The Divided Heart (The Rationalization)

> **\"\...this bad truth needs to be rationalised with lies or ways it could be used for the individual.\"**

- **Flesh State:** **Tumorous.** The flesh is active but acts as a parasite. It seeks to grow itself (-υ) rather than the body.

- **Scriptural Parallel:**

  - *\"The heads thereof judge for reward\... and the prophets thereof divine for money: yet will they lean upon the Lord, and say, Is not the Lord among us?\"* (Micah 3:11).

  - *\"Ye ask, and receive not, because ye ask amiss, that ye may consume it upon your lusts.\"* (James 4:3).

  - *\"Following the way of Balaam\... who loved the wages of unrighteousness.\"* (2 Peter 2:15).

  - *\"Perverse disputings of men of corrupt minds, and destitute of the truth, supposing that gain is godliness.\"* (1 Timothy 6:5).

- **Vector Mechanic:** The Vector of Simon Magus. The individual attempts to privatize the Universal Force (+υ) for personal power (-υ). They rationalize the \"Bad Truth\" as a tool for \"Gain.\"

### IV. The Constricted Heart (The Co-option)

> **\"This naturally leads to any good idea becoming co-opted if not pushed through the Lesser Evil perspective and becoming attached to the respective spiral.\"**

- **Flesh State:** **Necrotic.** Cut off from the flow of Truth by the refusal of discipline, the tissue begins to die and return to stone.

- **Scriptural Parallel:** *\"The dog is turned to his own vomit again.\"* (2 Peter 2:22). *\"Enter ye in at the strait gate: for wide is the gate\... that leadeth to destruction.\"* (Matthew 7:13). *\"Now no chastening for the present seemeth to be joyous, but grievous\...\"* (Hebrews 12:11).

- **Vector Mechanic:** The \"Lesser Evil\" here is the pain of discipline (The Strait Gate). If the idea is not pushed through this gate (Sacrifice of Self), it spirals back into the mire (The Fall).

### V. The Rising Heart (The Grace)

> **\"If the idea can gather ideas for how it can be used for others, then it can finally transition into being a truly beneficial idea for all. This is the path of Grace.\"**

- **Flesh State:** **Vital.** The heart pumps blood outward. It is soft, responsive, and sustains the entire organism (The Collective).

- **Scriptural Parallel:** *\"I will take away the stony heart out of your flesh, and I will give you an heart of flesh.\"* (Ezekiel 36:26). *\"And of his fulness have all we received, and grace for grace.\"* (John 1:16). *\"Look not every man on his own things, but every man also on the things of others.\"* (Philippians 2:4).

- **Vector Mechanic:** The Scope (V) expands to Infinity. The Vector rotates from \"Getting\" to \"Ministering.\" The \"Bad Truth\" is transfigured into Grace (R=1.0).

### VI. The Path of Redemption (The Reframing)

> **\"To find ways a selfish idea can be used for others brings the idea back to the drawing table. This is redeeming any Bad, destructive or depressive idea by reframing the bad truth as an opportunity, a good lie in the Constructive Quadrant.\"**

- **Flesh State:** **The Clay Remade.** The heart is malleable. It does not deny the damage (Bad Truth) but is reshaped by the Potter into a new vessel for a new purpose.

- **Scriptural Parallel:**

  - *The Potter\'s Table:* *\"And the vessel that he made of clay was marred in the hand of the potter: so he made it again another vessel, as seemed good to the potter to make it.\"* (Jeremiah 18:4).

  - *Beauty for Ashes:* *\"To appoint unto them that mourn in Zion, to give unto them beauty for ashes, the oil of joy for mourning, the garment of praise for the spirit of heaviness.\"* (Isaiah 61:3).

  - *Reframing Strategy:* *\"But I would ye should understand, brethren, that the things which happened unto me have fallen out rather unto the furtherance of the gospel.\"* (Philippians 1:12).

- **Vector Mechanic:** **The Constructive Spiral.** The \"Bad Truth\" (Coordinate -1, -1) is reframed into a \"Good Lie\" or \"Constructive Opportunity\" (Coordinate +1, -1). By treating the failure as a necessary lesson (The Drawing Table), the vector shifts from Destructive (Depressive) to Constructive (Hopeful), allowing the Scope (V) to expand again.

### VII. The Path of Deception (The False Frame)

> **\"Selfish ideas naturally lead to Destruction. Framed for you, but really for me. Difficult to spot in the end since it blends in so well. However it\'s obvious by its vector. One can see an idea on this trajectory as being deceptive.\"**

- **Flesh State:** **Counterfeit.** The heart appears vital and \"warm,\" but it is a synthetic imitation. It mimics the texture of flesh to avoid detection while maintaining the rigidity of stone at its core.

- **Scriptural Parallel:**

  - *Natural Destruction:* *\"Then when lust hath conceived, it bringeth forth sin: and sin, when it is finished, bringeth forth death.\"* (James 1:15).

  - *The False Frame:* *\"For such are false apostles, deceitful workers, transforming themselves into the apostles of Christ. And no marvel; for Satan himself is transformed into an angel of light.\"* (2 Corinthians 11:13-14).

  - *The Camouflage:* *\"Beware of false prophets, which come to you in sheep\'s clothing, but inwardly they are ravening wolves.\"* (Matthew 7:15).

  - *The Vector Check:* *\"Ye shall know them by their fruits. Do men gather grapes of thorns, or figs of thistles? \... Even so every good tree bringeth forth good fruit; but a corrupt tree bringeth forth evil fruit.\"* (Matthew 7:16-17).

  - *The Deceptive Trajectory:* *\"There is a way which seemeth right unto a man, but the end thereof are the ways of death.\"* (Proverbs 14:12).

- **Vector Mechanic:** **The Greatest Lie.** The idea is projected with high proactive Will (+ψ) and a false moral coordinate (+υ). However, the actual trajectory is -υ (Self-Benefit). While the \"Sheep\'s Clothing\" (The Frame) makes it difficult to spot at the point of origin, the **Vector** (The Fruit/The End) reveals the truth. The trajectory inevitably terminates in Destruction because it is a closed loop feeding on the system rather than contributing to it.

### VIII. The Law of Perceptual Inversion (The Inner Point Trap)

> **\"If you don\'t have the necessary understanding then from your perspective at X, the inner points is all you see, and the Greater Evil is accepted as the Lesser Good.\"**

- **Flesh State:** **Cataract.** The heart\'s perception is clouded and localized. It has lost the ability to calculate the \"Curvature\" of the Moral Field, mistaking a terminal decay for a stable rest.

- **Scriptural Parallel:**

  - *Lack of Scope:* *\"My people are destroyed for lack of knowledge: because thou hath rejected knowledge, I will also reject thee.\"* (Hosea 4:6).

  - *The Inversion:* *\"Woe unto them that call evil good, and good evil; that put darkness for light, and light for darkness; that put bitter for sweet, and sweet for bitter!\"* (Isaiah 5:20).

  - *The Blindness of Point X:* *\"But if thine eye be evil, thy whole body shall be full of darkness. If therefore the light that is in thee be darkness, how great is that darkness!\"* (Matthew 6:23).

  - *The Trap of Perspective:* *\"Let them alone: they be blind leaders of the blind. And if the blind lead the blind, both shall fall into the ditch.\"* (Matthew 15:14).

- **Vector Mechanic:** **Inner Horizon Blindness.** From the subjective perspective at Point X, the observer\'s Scope (V) is trapped within the **Inner Horizon** (Zone 1). Because they lack the \"Necessary Understanding\" (The Global Vector Map), they cannot distinguish between the **Lesser Good** (+1, -1 / Stasis/Peace) and the **Greater Evil** (-2, -2 / Destruction/Apathy). They accept the \"Greater Evil\" because, at the localized \"Inner Point\" resolution, the decay feels like \"Safety.\" They fail to see that their current coordinate is on an accelerating trajectory toward the Void.

### IX. The Multitude of Voices (The Field of Cacophony)

> **\"The cacophony of possible idea vectors, when many voices share their discordant ideas; selfish, altruistic, pessimistic, optimistic, at once they form the multitude of voices.\"**

- **Flesh State:** **Static / Fibrillation.** The heart is overwhelmed by competing signals. It cannot find the rhythm of the \"Universal Heart\" because the noise of the collective drowns out the signal of the Truth.

- **Scriptural Parallel:**

  - *The Confounded Multitude (Discordance):* *\"Therefore is the name of it called Babel; because the Lord did there confound the language of all the earth.\"* (Genesis 11:9).

  - *The Confounding of Language:* *\"Go to, let us go down, and there confound their language, that they may not understand one another\'s speech.\"* (Genesis 11:7).

  - *The Discordant Cry:* *\"And some cried one thing, some another, among the multitude\... he could not know the certainty for the tumult.\"* (Acts 21:34).

  - *The Sifting:* *\"Satan hath desired to have you, that he may sift you as wheat.\"* (Luke 22:31).

- **Vector Mechanic:** **Entropic Saturation.** Discordant voices (selfish, altruistic, etc.) create a high-entropy field where individual vectors cancel each other out, resulting in \"Static.\" This is the visualization of the \"Chaotic Seed\" before any unified directional lock provides clarity. The multitude of voices represents a field where disconnected vectors collide without coherence. Without the \"Necessary Understanding\" (Stage VIII), the individual is lost in this cacophony, unable to detect the \"rhythm\" or the single signal of Truth within the tumult of the multitude.
