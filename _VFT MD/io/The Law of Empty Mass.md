# The Law of Empty Mass (Theological Mechanics)

**Theorem:** *Any Good idea that starts with little actual mass and an observation will be used as cover for extractive ideas.*

This law defines the \"Path of the Fall from Grace\"---the specific trajectory where a truth is hollowed out to become a container for greed.

### I. The Hollow Observation (The Cover)

> **\"Any Good idea that starts with little actual mass and an observation\...\"**

- **Flesh State:** **The Mask.** It mimics the shape of a solution but lacks the density of sacrifice. It is a skin without a body.

- **Scriptural Parallel:** *Absalom\'s Politics.* \"And Absalom said\... See, thy matters are good and right; but there is no man deputed of the king to hear thee\... Oh that I were made judge in the land!\" (2 Samuel 15:3-4).

- **Vector Mechanic:** **High Velocity / Zero Mass.** The deceiver uses a \"High Fidelity Observation\" (The King is absent/The System is broken) to bypass resistance (k). Because there is \"little actual mass\" (no real plan/sacrifice), the idea creates no friction, only speed.

### II. The Extractive Turn (The Fall)

> **\"\...will be used as cover for extractive ideas. This is the path of the Fall from Grace.\"**

- **Flesh State:** **The Parasite.** It attaches to a host (the vulnerable victim) not to feed *them*, but to feed *on* them.

- **Scriptural Parallel:** *\"Clouds they are without water, carried about of winds; trees whose fruit withereth, without fruit, twice dead, plucked up by the roots.\"* (Jude 1:12).

- **Vector Mechanic:** **The Inversion.** The vector rotates from Service (\[me, you\]) to Extraction (\[me, me\]). The \"Cover\" (The Observation) remains visible, but the \"Payload\" (The Intent) becomes purely selfish (-υ).

### III. The Manifestations

> **\"You can spot this in ambition traps, scams and other cure-all/get rich quick schemes, love bombing, etc.\"**

- **Flesh State:** **The Mirage.** It promises water to the thirsty but delivers only sand.

- **Scriptural Parallel:** *\"For when they speak great swelling words of vanity, they allure through the lusts of the flesh\... While they promise them liberty, they themselves are the servants of corruption.\"* (2 Peter 2:18-19).

- **Vector Mechanic:** **The Ambition Trap.** The scammer targets the victim\'s \"Will to Power\" (+ψ). \"Love Bombing\" and \"Get Rich Quick\" schemes work by overloading the victim\'s desire, making them blind to the lack of mass.

### IV. The Algorithm of Deception

> **\"Identify problem, fake a solution. All while extracting personal benefit.\"**

- **Flesh State:** **The Cancer.** It identifies a wound in the body, pretends to be the clot that heals it, but instead drains the blood.

- **Scriptural Parallel:** *The Sin of Gehazi.* Identify Problem (Naaman is wealthy/Elisha took nothing) → Fake Solution (My master sent me for silver) → Extract Benefit (Hid it in the house). \"The leprosy therefore of Naaman shall cleave unto thee.\" (2 Kings 5:20-27).

- **Vector Mechanic:** **The Switch.**

  1.  **Identify Problem:** Establish Truth (t=1).

  2.  **Fake Solution:** Amplify Ideological Velocity (v_ideology) to hide the vacuum.

  3.  **Extract Benefit:** Harvest resources from the victim to the self.
