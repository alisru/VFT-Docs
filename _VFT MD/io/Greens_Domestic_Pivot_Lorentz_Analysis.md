## THE POLICY SUITE

### 1. National Energy Network (NEN)
- **What:** Gov-owned solar on 10M homes
- **Result:** 100% elimination of electricity bills by 2031
- **Cost:** $93.5B (fiscally neutral, funded from $14.9B/year fossil fuel subsidies)
- **Jobs:** 150K direct + 300K indirect
- **Timeline:** Starts 2026, universal relief 2030

### 2. Emergency Housing Acceleration (EHAP)
- **What:** 60-day approvals (vs 12-24 months)
- **How:** Council-led precinct planning
- **Cost Reduction:** $100k-$150k per affordable dwelling
- **Subsidies:** $20k/unit modular, $15k Passive House
- **Funded:** HAFF returns (~$500M/year)

### 3. Parent-Child Digital Safety Link (PCDSL)
- **What:** Gov registry for kids' devices, mandatory platform controls
- **vs Labor:** Sophisticated tools vs blunt under-16 ban
- **Privacy:** Only registered devices (not all users)
- **Appeal:** Suburban parents, swing voters
