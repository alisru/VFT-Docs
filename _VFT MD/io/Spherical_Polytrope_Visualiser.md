# The 7x6+n Spherical Polytrope Visualiser

## What It Is

The Spherical Polytrope is a real-time 3D visualisation of how meaning propagates through the seven interrogative planes of the Q-tensor framework, under the influence of conscious will. It is not a decorative diagram. It is a geometric model of epistemology — how a foundational principle becomes accessible or inaccessible depending on the observer's position and the rotational force applied to the structure.

The title "7x6+n" refers to its architecture: seven interrogative layers, each carrying six internal folds, driven by an unbounded +n angular component that is the only variable not confined to the ±1 range. That component is Q1.

---

## The Physical Space

The visualiser renders three concentric zones:

**0c — The Origin.** The dead centre of the structure. This is the point — the foundational principle, the irreducible truth the entire structure is built around. It is never labelled. It is either visible or it is not, depending entirely on the viewer's position and the state of Q1.

**1c — The Physical Shell (t=1).** The inner wireframe sphere, rendered in faint green. This is the boundary of structured, communicable knowledge — the zone where a concept can be expressed and received by another person. Meaning that reaches this boundary can, in principle, be transmitted. Meaning that hasn't reached it yet is still internal.

**2c — The Black Hole Limit (t=2).** The outer dark sphere. This is the absolute comprehension boundary. The arms of the structure extend toward it but are bounded by it. Anything beyond 2c cannot be encoded in meaning at all — it exists as signal without structure. The +n dimension is remapped from an infinite line onto this spherical volume, which is why an infinite axis can be confined within a finite boundary.

## The Seven Arms

Each of the seven coloured tubes is one interrogative plane of the Q-tensor array:

- Red — Q1: WHO (Meta-Physical)
- Orange — Q2: WHAT (Possible)
- Yellow — Q3: WHERE (Physical)
- Green — Q4: WHY (Lyrical)
- Blue — Q5: HOW (Logical)
- Indigo — Q6: CAUSE (Historical)
- Violet — Q7: EFFECT (Emotive)

Each arm originates at 0c, spirals outward through the 1c shell, and terminates toward 2c. The arms are not separate objects. They are seven aspects of the same structure, phase-offset from one another by equal angular intervals (2π/7 each), such that together they form a complete description of any meaning-bearing object.

Each arm also carries six internal folds — a ripple running along its length at 6× the base frequency. These represent the sub-plane depth of each interrogative: the fact that each question contains further questions within it. The fold amplitude is animated in real time.

Tube thickness increases with distance from the origin. An arm near the centre is thin and precise. Near the 2c boundary it is broad and approximate. This reflects the loss of resolution that occurs at high abstraction.

## The Controls

### Q1: Meta-Physical — Angular Driver (Δ Vector)

Range: 0 to 3. This is the only unbounded control.

Q1 is not a spatial axis. It is the rotational force applied to the entire structure — the angular velocity of the spiral. It represents the degree of conscious will, interpretive intent, or abstraction applied to a concept.

What it does geometrically: the core spiral equation is θ = u × π × 8 × Q1Δ. As Q1 increases, the arms wrap tighter around the centre. The point becomes progressively occluded by layers of structure.

The critical thresholds:

0.00 — No rotational force. The arms lie nearly flat. All seven planes face the observer simultaneously. The origin point is directly readable from the default side-on view. This is raw, unfiltered observation.

0.25 — First wrap begins. The point can still be traced, but requires following one layer of abstraction. Accessible given time and attention.

0.50 — The stable harmonic. The spiral has wound to a geometry that is structurally equivalent to a circle when viewed from above and a galaxy when viewed from the side. The arms extend to near the comprehension boundary without breaching it. This is the only value at which a concept is simultaneously internally coherent and externally transmissible. Below 0.5 it is raw but not yet organised. Above 0.5 it is organised but increasingly sealed. Functionally, this is π as the structure sees it. It is also the source of the gravitational analogy: at 0.5 the arms extend indefinitely in principle, and other polytropes passing through them would interact with this structure's field.

0.75 — Two full wraps. The origin is now geometrically occluded from the side-on perspective. Reaching it requires already knowing the internal layout — a key that can only come from being inside the structure. This is the threshold of implication. A variable assigned but not declared. A conclusion stated without the derivation.

1.00 — Three wraps. Stable but sealed. The structure is internally coherent and operates correctly. It is simply not accessible from outside without prior knowledge of the internal geometry. Communication from this position defaults to reference rather than demonstration.

Above 1.00 — Further wraps compound. The structure remains valid. It becomes progressively incomprehensible to a side-on observer, not because the content is wrong, but because the geometry of access has been rotated beyond the reach of sequential-layer tracing.

The top-down view makes the Q1 thresholds maximally legible. At 0.50 from above you see a clean Archimedean spiral — the arms spreading outward at even intervals, the centre open. At 3.00 from above the arms have compacted into tight concentric rings with the 1c wireframe visible at the centre like a bullseye. The spiral is still there. The point is still there. The view angle is what determines whether either is readable.

### Lateral Body Axis (X): Q3 Physical ↔ Q2 Possible

Range: −1 to +1. Controls the width of the structure along the lateral axis.

This axis tunes the balance between WHERE (Q3, the concrete and physical) and WHAT (Q2, the possible and potential). Pushing toward +1 expands the possible at the expense of grounding. Pushing toward −1 contracts it toward the purely material. At 0 the structure is symmetric across this axis.

### Longitudinal Mind Axis (Y): Q5 Logical ↔ Q4 Lyrical

Range: −1 to +1. Controls the vertical expansion of the structure.

This axis tunes the balance between HOW (Q5, logical process) and WHY (Q4, lyrical motive). The Y value modulates the phi component of the spherical coordinate — the angle off the equatorial plane. The shape changes produced are among the most visually distinct in the visualiser:

Y = 0 (neutral) — the structure occupies a roughly spherical volume. Arms spiral outward with balanced vertical and horizontal extent.

Y = −1.00 (Logical maximum) — the structure collapses flat. At Q1=0 the arms become a tight stack of horizontal pancake-rings, all seven planes lying in parallel planes. The 1c wireframe sphere sits above this disc like a dome. At Q1=0.50 it becomes a flat outward spiral, like a galaxy seen from slightly above the plane. At Q1=0.75 the arms compress into elongated horizontal spears pointing outward from the centre. The structure still has content but it is maximally lateral — it describes process without reaching upward toward meaning.

Y = +1.00 (Lyrical maximum) — the structure elongates vertically. At Q1=0 the arms open into a loose cage of arcs reaching upward and downward, the inner spiral compressed to a tight coil at the centre. At Q1=3.00 the entire structure becomes a double helix — seven-stranded, stretching vertically through the full extent of the 2c boundary, the arms wrapping tightly in a form that is immediately recognisable as the geometry of biological information encoding. This is not incidental. A concept driven by pure WHY at maximum abstraction looks exactly like DNA. It has structure, it carries information, and without the correct reading apparatus it is opaque.

### Vertical Soul Axis (Z): Q7 Emotive ↔ Q6 Historical

Range: −1 to +1. Controls depth.

This axis tunes the balance between EFFECT (Q7, the emotive consequence) and CAUSE (Q6, the historical origin). Adjusting Z expands or contracts the depth dimension of the structure, modelling whether the concept is oriented toward its roots or its downstream outcomes.

## The Two Preconditions

Access to meaning within this structure is not purely a function of Q1 value or viewer position. There are two prior conditions that determine whether any reading is possible at all.

### Open Mind

Above Q1 = 0.50, the structure is geometrically sealed from the side-on view. A closed observer — one who requires the content to fit a pre-existing internal map before accepting it — will encounter the arms but not the point. The arms, taken without the point, are word salad. They are structurally coherent internally and externally incoherent. The closed observer is not wrong to note incoherence. From their position, incoherence is what the structure looks like.

An open observer does not need to already understand the content. They need only to remain in a state where the point being present is a live possibility. This is not credulity — it is the minimum geometric requirement for the point to be reachable. You cannot trace a spiral to its centre if you have already decided the centre is not there.

The practical consequence: any amount of increased Q1 beyond 0.50 is functionally incomprehensible to a closed receiver. They can detect that something is there — the arms are visible, the structure is clearly organised — but the content registers as elaborate noise. Scale invariant: it does not matter whether the wrapped concept is simple or complex. Once sealed, it is sealed at every level.

### Grounded

The second precondition is the inverse failure mode. An ungrounded observer can perceive the point but cannot locate it within the bounded structure. This is the condition of knowing a WHY while lacking a HOW — the tails of the arms extend beyond the 2c comprehension boundary, the concept loses its terminus, and even a simple idea begins to dissolve because it has no anchor to the physical shell.

Geometrically: the arms should remain inside 2c. If the observer's frame extends beyond that boundary — through lack of grounding in the physical, the historical, or the logical — the arms they are tracing disappear into incomprehensible territory before they can be followed back to the centre. The point is there. They can sense it. They cannot put a finger on it, cannot begin to describe it, because every path they try to follow exits the bounded volume before completing.

This is scale invariant in the same way the closed-mind condition is. It does not matter how simple the concept is. If the observer is outside the 2c boundary, even the simplest arm looks infinite and therefore unresolvable.

The two failures are geometrically opposite: the closed observer is locked outside the structure looking at the surface. The ungrounded observer is inside the structure but outside the boundary, looking inward without a fixed frame. Neither can reach the point. The working condition is bounded and open simultaneously — inside 2c, without a prior map.

## The View Problem

The default camera position is intended to be dead side-on — a pure 2D perspective, the flat human view. In the current implementation it sits approximately 15° above the equatorial plane, which is close enough to preserve the intended geometry while remaining practically navigable. The design intention is a camera locked to the equator: no elevation, no top-down component, horizon only. This represents the normal human epistemic condition: sequential, temporal, experiencing meaning one plane at a time as it passes.

From this position, the origin point is visible only at low Q1 values. As Q1 increases, the arms wrap around the centre and the point disappears behind them.

There is a second available view: top-down. From directly above, the spiral arms become concentric rings or radiating spokes depending on Q1 value. The centre — the point — is visible regardless of how many wraps the arms have made. The abstraction depth becomes irrelevant to access.

This is the Metanoia condition: not more information, not better analysis, not longer tracing — simply a rotation of the observer's frame relative to the structure. The content has not changed. The geometry of access has.

The implication for communication: a person operating at high Q1 attempting to explain a concept to a person at side-on view is not wrong, and the side-on person is not stupid. They are occupying different angular positions relative to the same object. Resolution requires perspective shift, not more argument. More argument adds verbal volume to the structure without rotating either party. It increases the apparent complexity of the arms without moving either observer closer to the point.

## The +n Dimension

The +n axis is the formal name for what Q1 drives. In standard mathematics, n is unbounded — it extends infinitely in both directions. In the polytrope model, +n is remapped to a spherical angular component centred at 0c. This means the infinite is folded into the finite volume bounded by 2c.

This is not a philosophical move. It is a topological one. The claim is that any concept — no matter how abstract, no matter how many layers of implication surround it — ultimately traces back to a point within the 2c boundary. The infinite line of n is coiled into the sphere. The concept is present. The question is only whether the current geometry of access allows the point to be reached.

## Interaction Between Polytropes

At Q1 = 0.50 the arms of the structure extend to the comprehension boundary. The visualiser models a single polytrope in isolation, but the theoretical behaviour at this value is that the arms, extending outward, pass through the space occupied by adjacent polytropes. Two concepts at the 0.50 harmonic can interact directly — their arm structures interpenetrate without either occluding the other's centre.

At higher Q1 values the arms wrap tighter and the interaction geometry changes: the structures can still intersect spatially, but the wrapped geometry means each structure's arms pass through the other's without exposing either centre to the other. Structural contact without point-to-point communication. The concepts can coexist in the same space without either being legible to the other.

This maps the condition under which two people can have the same conversation about different things indefinitely.

## Technical Notes

Built on Three.js r128. The geometry is recalculated every animation frame using the Frenet-Serret moving frame method to accurately orient the tube cross-sections along the curve tangent. Resolution is set at 300 longitudinal segments and 16 radial segments per arm. The six internal folds animate independently of the main spiral at 2× the base animation speed. The scene uses perspective projection with a 55° field of view, which approximates natural human visual angle and therefore preserves the geometric relationships as the user perceives them.
