# Alisru's Normal Alethekanon Hegemonic Overview of Life Evaluation (A.N.A.H.O.L.E. Test) V15.0

## 1. Core Mechanics: The Objective/Subjective Delta Protocol

This version finalizes the instrument's architecture. Its core diagnostic is the measurement of the delta between a user's **Ideal Self** (what they believe they *should* do) and their **Pragmatic Self** (what they predict they *would* actually do), all within a clearly defined **Objective Frame**.

- **Objective Frame:** The inherent nature of the question itself (e.g., a "Bad Truth," a "Good Lie," a "Neutral Preference"). This is the unchanging reality of the scenario.

- **Subjective Frame:** The user's response to that reality.

This allows the instrument to map not just the user's choices, but the interaction between their internal worldview and an external, immutable context.

### 2. The Internal Mapping System

The internal system maps two primary vectors for the user: the **Ideal Vector** ("Should") and the **Pragmatic Vector** ("Would"). The relationship between these two vectors, within the context of the question's Objective Frame, is the primary data point.

## 5. Scoring and Analysis (Expanded)

The final analysis is now significantly more powerful.

- **The Ideal-Pragmatic Delta (The "Hypocrisy Gap"):** This is the core metric. The instrument calculates the vector distance between the user's Ideal and Pragmatic coordinates. A large delta reveals a significant conflict between the user's principles and their perceived self-control.

- **Archetype Profile:** A Primary and Secondary Archetype is now calculated for the user's **Ideal Self** and their **Pragmatic Self** separately, providing a nuanced profile (e.g., an Idealist in principle, but a Pragmatist in practice).

- **Cognitive Gaps:** The gaps between the user's pragmatic actions and their projections of their Tribe and Society are calculated to map their sense of social alignment or alienation.
